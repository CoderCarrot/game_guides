Squazzil

No matter what has happened -- disaster, illness, family tragedy -- Squazzils are always bright-eyed and bushy-tailed. It can get really annoying.

Level

4

Base Value

1000 coins

Housing

Squazzil Home

Animal

Squirrel

Candy

Swizzle Stick

Attack Type

Conkers
A Squazzil is an orange and yellow squirrel piñata. It has orange irises. It has a somewhat thick tail. It has orange patterns on its body.

Got a Hazelnut Tree? In that case you've probably got a few Squazzils too. These energetic little critters are unlikely to cause trouble, but fence them off before letting a Twingersnap into your garden or you risk mass panic.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Level 3 piñata in the garden.
  • No Twingersnaps in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have a Hazelnut Tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Hazelnuts.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Blackberries.
  • Have a Squazzil house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Snapdragon flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Watercress Seed.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Hazel Tree in the Garden.

Visit Requirements

  • There are 3 hazelnuts in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 hazelnuts.

Romance requirements

  • Has eaten 3 sunflower seeds

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Hazel tree in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have any level 3 Piñata resident.
  • No Twingersnaps resident in the garden.

Visit

  • Have 1 Hazelnut Tree grown in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 3 Hazelnuts.

Romance

  • Has eaten 2 Blackberries.
  • Have a Squazzil house in the garden.

Variant (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily Seed.

Squazzil Uses

  • Eating 1 Squazzil helps to meet the Twingersnap's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Squazzil helps to meet the Badgesicle's Romance requirements.

Other Information

  • Squazzils will stay away from the garden when a Twingersnap or Badgesicle is resident.
  • Squazzils dislike being watered from the watering can.
  • Squazzils can climb trees.

Housing

The Squazzil Home is a giant acorn in an oak tree.

Rumors

  • A rumor from Leafos says that Squazzils were formerly called "Nonsquirrelles" before the player arrived.

Trivia

  • The Squazzil resembles Nabnut the squirrel from the Banjo-Kazooie series, as well as Conker from Conker's Bad Fur Day.
  • The Squazzil is the only piñata to have a tree as a just for fun attraction requirement.
