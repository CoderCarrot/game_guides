Pretztail

Well known for being sneaky and crafty, this animal likes to lurk about after the sun goes down. The Pretztail is a small predator, and you can keep one in your garden without being too much of a pest.

Level

Housing

Animal

Attack Type

A Pretztail is an orange fox piñata with yellow rings on its body. Its tail and ears are tipped with white, and its ears resemble candy corn. It has brown irises.

Some Piñatas can't help the way they are. The dusk-dwelling Pretztail may make short work of your early Piñata rearing with a huge appetite for Quackberries, Cluckles and Bunnycombs, but there's no malice in it.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Nighttime in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • There is 1 Cluckles in the garden OR there is 1 Bunnycomb in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Cluckles OR has eaten 1 Bunnycomb.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Quackberry.
  • There is a Pretztail house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a bluebell.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a water lily flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a gooseberry fool.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Bone evolves it into a Pieena.

Appear Requirements

  • Nighttime in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Tartridge in the garden OR have 1 Bunnycomb in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Tartridge OR has eaten 1 Bunnycomb.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Cluckles.
  • There is a Pretztail house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Fool.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell.

Trick Requirements
