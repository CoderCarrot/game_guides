Swanana

Sophisticated, refined, almost regal, you may think the Swanana is a fine-looking bird, but you'd be wrong. The Swanana prefers the term 'gorgeous!'

Level

7

Base Value

2800 coins

Housing

Swanana Home

Animal

Mute Swan

Candy

Banana

Attack Type

Lifesaver
The Swanana is a white swan piñata. It is very sophisticated and loves to show itself off, and is considered the queen of ponds, as it’s always fighting with its ever-persistent rivals, the Quackberry’s and Juicygeese.

The Swanana appears to be very clean, and even highly egotistical. They never get along with other similar species, mainly because they’re jealous and love a good fight.

Appearance

Its beak is orange, it has a dark blue mask pattern on its face, and it has blue irises. It has a pointy little tail, small white wings which have a light blue, and dark blue pattern on them. It also has a light blue zig-zag pattern on its neck, and blue feet.

The Swanana believes that grace and class allow it the right to expect a certain standard of living, often involving jewelry and hand-delivered sandwiches. It won't even visit until you're practically a veteran.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 27 gardener or better.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 200 square pinometers of water (20%).
  • Garden is worth 20,000 Chocolate Coins.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 250 square pinometers of water (25%).
  • Has eaten 2 Sandwiches.

Romance Requirements

  • You have a Swan Fountain in the garden.
  • Is wearing a diamond choker.
  • Has eaten 3 water lily flowers.
  • You have 300 square pinometers of water (30%).
  • Have a Swanana house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Snapdragon flower.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 27 gardener or higher.

Unblock requirements

  • You paid a Weedling to remove the Weeds.

Visit requirements

  • Theres a Swan Fountain in the Garden.
  • The garden is worth 20.000 Chocolate Coins.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 25 Square pinometers water.
  • Has eaten two Sandwiches.

Romance requirements

  • Is wearing a Diamond Choker.
  • Has eaten 3 Water Lilys.
  • You have 30 square pinometers water.
  • There is a Swanana Home in your garden.

Variant 1 requirements (pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Variant 2 requirements (green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Seed.

Variant 3 requirements (purple)

  • Has eaten Snapdragon flower.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Gooseberry to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Newtgat to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Cowlick
  • Tail feathers
  • Neck feather frills

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Cherrapin resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 8 gardener or better.

Visit

  • Have 200 pinometers of water (20%).
  • Garden is worth 20,000 chocolate coins.

Resident

  • Has eaten 2 Sandwiches.
  • Have 250 pinometers of water (25%).

Romance

  • Has eaten 3 Waterlilies.
  • Have 300 pinometers of water (30%).
  • Have a Swanana Fountain in the garden.
  • Have a Swanana house in the garden.

Variant (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Swanana Uses

  • Eating 1 Swanana helps meet the Sour Cocoadile's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Swanana helps meet the Cocoadile's Romance requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • A Swanana resident is required to unlock the Mystery Home. (Original and Trouble in Paradise)
  • A Swanana Romance award is required to unlock the Mystery Home. (Pocket Paradise)
  • A Swanana can be Romanced with a Rashberry to produce a Pigxie after the Mystery Home has been purchased from Willy Builder.

In the TV Series

Svetlana Swanana

Svetlana Swanana is a character found in the Viva Piñata TV series. She is the date of Rory Rashberry, and gave birth to a Pigxie.
