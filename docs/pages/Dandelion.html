Dandelion

The dandelion was probably named such because its yellow flower looks like a Roario's mane. Who knew that it would have something else in common with the king of the beasts?

Type

Weed

Level

3

Base Value

6 coins
The Dandelion is a weed introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It can scare piñatas and make them run away. It is red like other weeds in the series, and has a bulb like structure on its head.

Appearance

The Dandelion appears as a red flower with a red bulb like flower head. It has orange strands that look like a lion's head.

Uses

Dandelion Flowerhead Uses

  • Feeding one Dandelion to a Sparrowmint meets the evolution requirements.
  • Feeding one Dandelion to a Mallowolf, Sherbat or a Bunnycomb meets the trick requirements.
  • Feeding 4 Dandelions to a Moozipan meets the romance requirements.
  • Feeding a Dandelion to a Polollybear turns it into a yellow variant.

Dandelion Seed Uses

  • Feeding a Dandelion seed to a Mallowolf, Sherbat or a Bunnycomb meets the trick requirements.
