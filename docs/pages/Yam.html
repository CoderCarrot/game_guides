Yam

The yam grows in sandy soil. Without this vegetable, many desert Piñatas would not survive the hostile environment. I'd recommend it baked then mashed with butter.

Type

Vegetable

Level

2

Base Value

30 coins

Fertilizer

Orange
Yam is an orange vegetable introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is a desert plant as well, needing either dirt or sand to grow. It can be tinkered into Crisps, used as bait for Sweetles or for romancing and variants.
