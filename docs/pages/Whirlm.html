Every garden has to start somewhere, and it's harder to keep Whirlms out than it is to keep them in. Ancient wisdom says: 'Whirlms are the foundation of any successful garden'. Please don't confuse them for stomach parasites.

The Whirlm is an orange worm piñata in Viva Piñata. It is the first piñata that will be encountered in the games.

Like an actual worm, it will crawl around a garden.

The Whirlm is well known for repeatedly entering a garden and becoming a resident due to its incredibly simple visiting and resident requirements, where it only needs 10 square pinometers of soil or grass to be satisfied with a garden.

Appearance

It is very small and orange in color. Around its eyes are lots of green triangles and it has red eyelids and blue irises. It has a red band about a third of the way down its body. A little ways further down, it has a few green circle patterns, then a thicker red stripe with a lighter zig-zag pattern through it.

Whirlm Uses

  • Having 1 Whirlm in the garden helps to meet the Sparrowmint's Appear requirements.
  • Having 2 Whirlms in the garden helps to meet the Sparrowmint's Visit requirements.
  • Having 4 Whirlms in the garden helps to meet the Fudgehog's Visit requirements.
  • Eating 1 Whirlm helps to meet the Sparrowmint's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Whirlm helps to meet the Profitamole's Resident requirements.
  • Eating 2 Whirlms helps to meet the Fudgehog's Resident requirements.
  • Having the Whirlm Romancer Award helps to meet the Sparrowmint's Resident requirements.
  • Having 3 Whirlms in the garden helps to meet the Profitamole's Visit requirements. (TiP only)
  • Eating 2 Whirlms helps to meet the Cluckles' Romance requirements. (TiP only)
  • Eating 1 Whirlm helps to meet the Roario's Trick 2 requirements. (TiP only)

Other Information

Whirlms like being watered from the watering can.

Housing

The Whirlm Home is made up of a lot of connected pipes that lead to an underground den.

In the TV Series

Wilson Whirlm

Wilson Whirlm is the central character of the TV series episode Whirlm with a Dream, in which Wilson dreams of being launched to a party. He eventually manages to get launched from the Cannoñata with a group as a whole disguised as Fergy Fudgehog.

Wendel Whilrm

Wendel Whirlm is a major character in the TV series episode Soil and Green. He tills the land so that plants can grow in his garden.
