Oak Tree

The most impressive, mighty and enormous tree you will ever see in your garden. Be warned that you need patience, care and lots of water before you will ever see the oak at its full potential.

The Oak Tree is the largest and most expensive tree to grow in the Viva Piñata series. It produces Acorns.

The oak tree needs to be fertilized with Green Fertilizer at 3 to 4 hour intervals, beginning with the 2nd growth burst, which takes place between 6 and 8 hours after planting, depending on the time of day you plant. The oak tree has 5 growth bursts, the first and last of which you cannot fertilize. The period of fertilization with the oak tree lasts from the appearance of green buds at the tips of the branches until the disappearance of the falling leaves that burst from those buds. The entire tree takes about 17 hours to grow fully. The oak tree will attract Eaglairs too.

Lots of piñata species are marked on the Oak Tree, including the Elephanilla.

Costolot's Store will only sell Oak Trees starting at Gardener Level 37 to get an Oak Tree, but Oak Tree seeds can be obtained from the Mine at a lower level.

Produce

The oak tree produces acorns. Acorns sell for 110 chocolate coins at Costolot's.
