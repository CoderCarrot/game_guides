Appearance

Its beak is yellow, and it has brown irises. It has a pointy little tail, small green wings, a white and green pattern on its belly, and yellow feet. It has a slight cowlick on the back of its head.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Gooseberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Appear Requirements

  • You have 3 square pinometers of water (3%).

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 corn plant or piece of corn in the garden.
  • You have 4 square pinometers of water (4%).

Resident Requirements

  • You have 4 square pinometers of water (4%).
  • Has eaten 1 piece of Bread.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Bluebell flowers.
  • There is a Quackberry house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

  • Has eaten a Bird of Paradise Seed.

Quackberry Uses

  • Eating 1 Quackberry helps to meet the Pretztail's Romance requirements.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Gooseberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Appear Requirements

  • You have 3 square pinometers of water (3%).

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 corn plant in the garden.
  • You have 4 square pinometers of water (4%)

Resident Requirements

  • You have 4 square pinometers of water (4%)
  • Has eaten 1 piece of Bread.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Custacean.
  • You have 4 square pinometers of water (4%)
  • There is a Quackberry house in the garden.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Evolution

  • Feeding it a Gooseberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Appear

  • Have 40 pinometers of water (4%).

Visit

  • Have 40 pinometers of water (4%).

Resident

  • Have 40 pinometers of water (4%).
  • Has eaten 1 piece of Bread.

Romance

  • Has eaten 2 Bluebells.
  • Have a Quackberry house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Snapdragon flower.

Quackberry Uses

  • Having 5 Quackberries in the garden helps to meet the Cocoadile's Visit requirements. (Trouble in Paradise and Pocket Paradise)
  • Having 6 Quackberries in the garden helps to meet the Cocoadile's Visit requirements. (Original)
  • Eating 1 Quackberry helps to meet the Pretztail's Romance requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Quackberry helps to meet the Cocoadile's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 3 Quackberries helps to meet the Cocoadile's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)

In the TV Series

Dr. Quackberry

Dr. Quincy Quackberry is a major character of the Viva Piñata TV series. He is a psychologist and is commonly seen dealing with other piñatas' health issues such as Confetti-itis.
