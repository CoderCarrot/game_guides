Parmadillo

The Parmadillo is very well prepared. It has a hard shell for protection, and it can roll up into an impregnable ball. No-one knows what this preparation is for, and the Parmadillo seems reluctant to spill the beans.

Level

The Parmadillo is an armadillo piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It also appears in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise. In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise it is the evolved form of the Fudgehog.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a coconut to a Fudgehog turns it into a Parmadillo, earning the player an achievement.

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding a coconut to a Fudgehog turns it into a Parmadillo. (Parmadillo Resident Requiremnts (TiP).jpg)

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Orange Tree seed.
  • You have 5 square pinometers of sand in the garden (0.5%).
  • Have a Parmadillo house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it a Hemlock Flower changes its color to white.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Medicine changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Banana changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Palm Tree seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a coconut to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Three bumps on head
  • Two long horns
  • Bumps on shell

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Feed a Coconut to a Fudgehog to transform it into a Parmadillo

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have 100 pinometers of dirt (10%).
  • You are a level 5 gardener or better.

Visit

  • Have 150 pinometers of dirt (15%).
  • There are 2 Raisants in the garden.
  • There are 2 Arocknids in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • Has eaten 1 Arocknid.
  • Has eaten 1 Taffly.

Romance

  • Has eaten 2 Bispotti.
  • Have a Parmadillo house in the garden.

Variant (Green)

  • Has eaten a Poison Ivy Seed.

Parmadillo Uses

  • Eating 3 Parmadillos meets the Sour Limeoceros's Resident requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Something I've noticed while taming Sour Limoceri: if you view the info of the Parmadillo they've eaten after they reconstruct outside of your garden, they appear to have the beginning of unused resident requirements involving Newtgats. Is this a glitch?

Like the Chocstrich, Pieena, Moojoo and Polollybear, this is another evolved pinata that has a resident requirement chart. If you make the Parmadillo unresident, view the wild Parmadillo and check the Resident requirements before it disappears. It has to do something with a Newtgat. You won't get the same Parmadillo to reside, however.
