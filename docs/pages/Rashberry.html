Rashberry

Round and wobbly, pink and jolly, a Rashberry is an animal that hasn't realized just how tasty it looks. When it's scared, it makes a cool squealing noise, which only encourages cruel people.

The Rashberry is a purple pig piñata.

The Rashberry is a species that proves rotten food can be useful, if you can be sure to keep the Mallowolves out of your pen. If it romances with the graceful Swanana, it might produce some interesting results.

Requirements (Original)

The Rashberry cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 1650 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 12. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 rotten hazelnut.
  • Has eaten 1 rotten chili.
  • Has eaten 1 rotten gooseberry.
  • Have a Rashberry house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Watercress Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a Poppy Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Nightshade Berry.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Cheesecake evolves it into a Hoghurt.

Appear Requirements

  • Have a gooseberry bush in the garden.
  • Have a chili in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 gooseberries in the garden.
  • Have 2 chilis in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 rotten gooseberries. (Due to a glitch, fresh gooseberries also count)
  • Has eaten 2 rotten chilis.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 bowl of pea soup.
  • Has eaten 1 muffin.
  • Is wearing a Bling Nose Ring.
  • Have a Rashberry house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Watercress Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a Poppy Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Nightshade Berry.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick 1: Has eaten a Bullrush.
  • Trick 2: Has eaten an Acorn.

Wildcard Variants

  • Tusks
  • Tri-tipped ears
  • Long snout

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having rotten fruit of any type in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Rashberry cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 1500 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 5. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 rotten Hazelnut.
  • Has eaten 1 rotten Chili.
  • Has eaten 1 rotten Gooseberry.
  • Have a Rashberry house in the garden.

Variant (Red)

  • Has eaten a Poppy flower.

Rashberry Uses

  • Having 6 Rashberries in the garden helps to meet the Mallowolf's Visit requirements.
  • Eating 3 Rashberries helps to meet the Mallowolf's Resident requirements.
  • A Rashberry resident is required to unlock the Mystery Home. (Original and Trouble in Paradise)
  • A Rashberry Romance award is required to unlock the Mystery Home. (Pocket Paradise)
  • A Rashberry can be Romanced with a Swanana to produce a Pigxie after the Mystery Home has been purchased from Willy Builder.

Other information

  • Rashberries tend to become unhappy very quickly. If you own a Rashberry, make sure you keep the garden drowned in Happy Candy and Joy Candy! Rashberries will eat Happy Candy on their own, but must be directed to eat Joy Candy. If your Rashberry is already very sad then you must offer it Happy Candies to raise its mood before you can give it a Joy Candy to cheer it all the way up, since sad piñata will not obey orders.
  • An easy way to keep the Rashberry satisfied is to let it wear a Rashberry Hat.
  • Rashberries like to be watered with the Watering Can.
  • Tapping on a fresh fruit with the shovel will turn it rotten, which can be helpful for meeting the Rashberry's requirements.

Rumors

  • An in-game rumor from Leafos claims that after it eats 10 pumpkins it becomes a Chippopatamus. (False)
  • An in-game rumor from Leafos claims that after it eats 10 Tafflies it becomes a Pigxie. (False)

Glitches and Errors

  • Due to a glitch, in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise, rotten or fresh gooseberries both count towards meeting the Rashberry's Resident requirements, even though only rotten ones are supposed to work.

In the TV Series

Rory Rashberry

Rory Rashberry is a character of the Viva Piñata TV series. He was a Pig Out Mountain explorer, helping Fergy Fudgehog and Paulie Pretztail reach the destination.


Commonly Asked Questions

Why won't my Rashberries eat Romance candy?

Make sure you've met their Romance requirements. If you haven't already romanced a pair of them once in that garden, they won't eat the candy. Make sure a predator (i.e. Mallowolf) isn't near the candy, which will scare them away from it. Try feeding them a Happy Candy if they're refusing out of sadness. A sad piñata won't obey any orders.
