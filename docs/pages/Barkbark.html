Barkbark

The Barkbark is man's best friend. His qualities are loyalty, cheery disposition, obedience, and every now and then slobbery over-friendliness.

Level

6

Base Value

2100 coins

Housing

Barkbark Home

Animal

Dog

Candy

Almond Bark

Attack Type

Squeaky toy
The Barkbark is a light blue dog piñata.

In Viva Piñata and Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise it is a domestic piñata that must be purchased from Paper Pets or Gretchen Fetchem's respectively. In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise it must be obtained from the wild.

For years, pooches like Barkbark have been called man's best friends. But when you start filling them with candy, that only sweetens the deal!

Appearance

Its irises are red and it has a large black nose. It has a lighter blue underbelly, lower head, and ear tips, when compared to the rest of the body. The Barkbark has a disproportionately large. angular head compared to the rest of its body.

Requirements (Original)

The Barkbark cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 2310 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 14. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing a Collar.
  • Has eaten 1 Bone.
  • There is a Barkbark house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Banana Split changes its color to yellow.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Bottle of Medicine changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a Poison Ivy flower changes its color to green.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Kittyfloss resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Barkbark cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 2100 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 6. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Bone.
  • Is wearing a Collar.
  • Have a Barkbark house in the garden.

Variant (Pink)

  • Has eaten 1 bottle of Medicine.

Barkbark Uses

  • A Barkbark equipped with a Spiked Collar can scare away Ruffians and Professor Pester. (Pocket Paradise)

Species Conflicts

Happiness Tips
