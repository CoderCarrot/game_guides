Fire weed

This plant didn't adapt to life in the Piñarctic regions. Instead, it chose to melt the snow and ice and burn a little place for itself. Old habits die hard, and this weed continues to scorch itself a spot, no matter how warm it is.

Type

Weed

Level

4

Base Value

10 coins
Fire weed is a weed plant introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. Unlike the real life plant it is based off, it can shoot fire ball projectiles that set piñatas on fire.

Appearance

The fire weed is a red plant that has three spray gland looking bulbs and blue hair like strands on top.
