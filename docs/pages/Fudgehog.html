Appearance

Its face is green, and has a darker green rectangle around its eyes as if it had a mask, and it has blue irises. The rest of the body are different colors, ranging from purple, red, blue, and yellow, in the order from how far away it is from the face. Its tail is a part of its body, and it has stubby small legs.

Unlike actual hedgehogs, they have a hump rather than quills, giving them a wobbly appearance.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • 4th night after you take over the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 4 Whirlms or 2 thistles in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Whirlms.
  • Has eaten 1 thistle.

Romance Requirements

  • Has drunk a bottle of milk.
  • There is a Fudgehog house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding Fudgehog a Chili changes its color to red.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding Fudgehog a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding Fudgehog Poison Ivy flower changes its color to green.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolve Requirements

  • Feed a Fudgehog a Coconut to evolve it into a Parmadillo.

  • Have 1 Holly Bush in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Holly Bush in the garden.
  • Have 1 Thistle in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Holly Berry.
  • Has eaten 1 Thistle Flower.
  • Have 1 Leaf Pile in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Has drunk a bottle of Milk.

In the TV Series

Fergy Fudgehog

Fergy Fudgehog is a main character in the Viva Piñata TV series. He does not like going to parties and is on a constant quest to avoid Langston Lickatoad as he is here to send both Fergy and Paulie Pretztail to a party.
