Ever wanted a Jameleon but found out they were too expensive? Your old Lickatoad is looking tired and out-of-date? Get a Newtgat-functions like a Lickatoad but with all that expensive Jameleon styling that you love.

Wellington boots
The Newtgat is a small pink newt/axolotl piñata. Its gills are red, and its large eyes have green irises. It has a purple tail, a pink and purple pattern covering its body, and purple legs.

The frilly, trilling Newtgat is a general pond favourite, sharing many traits with its nemesis the Lickatoad: a penchant for puddle-hopping, a low rank on the food chain and some interesting evolutionary potential.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Chili will evolve it into a Salamango.

Appear Requirements

  • You have 2 square pinometers of water (2%).

Visit Requirements

  • Have watercress growing in the garden.
  • You have 3 square pinometers of water (3%).

Resident Requirements

  • You have 3 square pinometers of water (3%).
  • Has eaten 3 watercress flowers.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • You have 3 square pinometers of water (3%).
  • Have a Newtgat house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a bird of paradise flower changes its color to orange.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily Seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell flower changes its color to blue.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have 20 pinometers of water (2%).
  • No Juicygeese resident in the garden.

Visit

  • Have Watercress growing in the garden.
  • Have 30 pinometers of water (3%).

Resident

  • Has eaten 3 Watercress flowers.
  • Have 30 pinometers of water (3%).

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • Have 30 pinometers of water (3%).
  • Have a Newtgat house in the garden.

Variant (Orange)

  • Has eaten a Bird of Paradise flower.

Newtgat Uses

  • Eating 1 Newtgat helps to meet the Badgesicle's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Newtgat helps to meet the Pengum's Resident requirements.
  • Eating 2 Newtgats helps to meet the Juicygoose's Romance requirements.
  • 1 Newtgat helps to meet the Parmadillo Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)

Housing

The Newtgat house looks like a Chinese palace lantern, or Dēnglóng.

Other Information

  • The Newtgat can climb trees.
  • Likes to be watered with the Watering Can.
  • If you are going to make a Newtgat garden, be careful of the Lickatoads that will come. Posssible solutions to avoid fighting include purchasing the Red Eye Rainbow from Ivor Bargain or putting Gas Masks from Paper Pets on the Lickatoads and Newtgats. Alternatively, making sure not to grow any flowers (Original and Pocket Paradise)/daisies and trees (TIP) will prevent Lickatoads from becoming residents in the first place.
