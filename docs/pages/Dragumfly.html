Dragumfly

The Dragumfly is a master of flight. It can hover over or skim the water, which is even more amazing when you consider how large it is. A little known fact is that its four wings and "Tally Ho!" call inspired 1940s pilots.

Level

7

Base Value

2800 coins

Housing

Dragumfly Home

Animal

Dragonfly

Candy

Gum

Attack Type

Helicopters
The Dragumfly is a rainbow dragonfly piñata. It has yellow and green markings on its head and thorax, with rectangular, hazel brown irises. It has rainbow multicolored rings going down its abdomen. The Dragumfly also has red legs, and long purple wings.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 26 gardener or better.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 15 square pinometers of water (15%).

Resident Requirements

  • Have 20 square pinometers of water (20%).
  • Have 12 bullrushes in the garden.
  • Has won a fight against a Redhott.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 25 square pinometers of water (25%).
  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • Has eaten 1 Buzzlegum.
  • Has eaten 1 snapdragon.
  • Have a Dragumfly house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a pumpkin changes its color to orange.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it poison ivy changes its color to green.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 26 gardener or better.

Unblock Requirements

  • Have 5 Reddhotts in your garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 15 square pinometers of water (15%).
Note: Will not visit the garden if it is raining

Resident Requirements

  • Have 20 square pinometers of water (20%).
  • Have 12 bullrushes in the garden.
  • Has won a fight against a Redhott.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 25 square pinometers of water (25%).
  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • Has eaten 1 Buzzlegum.
  • Has eaten 1 snapdragon.
  • Have a Dragumfly house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a pumpkin changes its color to orange.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it poison ivy changes its color to green.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick 1: Has eaten a Taffly.
  • Trick 2: Has eaten a White Flutterscotch.

Wildcard Variants

  • Ram-like horns
  • Rhino-like horns
  • Single rod-shaped crest on head

Requirements (Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Reddhott resident in your garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 7 gardener or better.

Visit

  • Have 150 pinometers of water. (15%)

Resident

  • Have 200 square pinometers of water. (20%)
  • Have 12 Bullrushes in the garden.
  • Has won a fight with a Reddhott.

Romance

  • Have 250 pinometers of water. (25%)
  • Has eaten 1 Raisant.
  • Has eaten 1 Buzzlegum.
  • Has eaten 1 Snapdragon flower.
  • Have a Dragumfly house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower.

Dragumfly Uses

  • Feeding a Dragumfly to a Dragonache helps meet its Maturation Requirements.

Errors

Other Information

  • The Dragumfly is the only piñata to not be spooked by predators and has the ability to frighten other piñatas, even their predators such as (Arocknids, Dragonaches, Lickatoads and Newtgats).
  • Dragumflies dislike being watered with the watering can.
