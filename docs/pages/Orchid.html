Classic

The orchid is a flower available to master gardeners (Level 35). To get one, buy its seed at Costolot's for 99 coins or receive one from Seedos. They must be planted in soil, grass, or long grass. Adding 3 bags of green fertilizer immediately after planting it will fully catalyze it and once fully catalyzed can be sold for 1,800 coins.

Uses

  • Feeding 3 orchids to a Zumbug will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding 4 orchids to a Galagoogoo will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding an orchid to a Bonboon will change it to its white variant. This works with Jameleons as well but the Jameleon will also have to eat a bottle of medicine.

Trouble in Paradise

The orchid is a flower available to master gardeners (Level 35). To get one, buy its seed at Costolot's for 99 coins or receive one from Seedos. They must be planted in soil, grass, or long grass. Adding 3 bags of green fertilizer immediately after planting it will fully catalyze it and once fully catalyzed can be sold for 1,800 coins.

Uses

  • Feeding 3 orchids to a Zumbug will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding 4 orchids to a galagoogoo will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding an orchid to a bonboon, custacean, or jameleon will change it to its white variant
  • Feeding an orchid to a chewnicorn will make it perform trick 1
  • Feeding an orchid to a Jeli will make it perform trick 2

Pocket Paradise

The orchid is a flower that becomes available once you become a level 6 gardener. To get one, buy its seed at Costolot's for 90 coins or receive one from Seedos. They must be planted in soil, grass, or long grass. Adding a bag of green fertilizer immediately after planting it will fully catalyze it and once catalyzed can be sold for 1,800 coins.

Uses

  • Having 5 orchids in your garden will help a limeoceros reach its visit requirements
  • Eating 5 orchids will help a limeoceros reach its resident requirements
  • Feeding 3 orchids to a zumbug will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding 4 orchids to a galagoogoo will help it reach its romance requirements
  • Feeding an orchid to a bonboon will turn it into a white colored variant
