Bispotti

This Piñata is very neat and tidy. It likes to buff its shell to a shine and lacquer its spots, and those eyelashes don't just naturally curl like that, you know? Unfortunately, all this attention on appearance means it often forgets to turn off the iron before it leaves the house in the morning.

The Bispotti is a pink and blue ladybug piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It also appears in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise.

This round little ladybird[sic] is one of the new kids on the block, and she’s quickly proving herself popular among the Piñata party set. Having a Bispotti at you birthday guarantees a good time for all!

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have any kind of flower in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have two flowers of any kind in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have three poppies in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Bispotti house in the garden.
  • Has eaten a Daisy flower.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a Watercress flower changes its color to green.

Variant 3 Requirements (White and Pink)

  • Feeding it a loaf of Bread (tinkered Corn) changes its color to white and pink.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a Holly Bush Seed.
  • Has eaten a bluebell flower.

Wildcard Variants

  • Curled antennas.
  • Sunflower-like frills around face.
  • Elytra are covered in bumps.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 2 Poppies in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 2 Poppies in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 3 Poppies in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 4 gardener or better.
  • There are 3 Poppies in the garden.

Visit

  • There are 5 Poppies in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 3 Poppy flowers.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Mushroom.
  • Have a Bispotti house in the garden.

Variant (Green)

  • Has eaten a Buttercup flower.

Bispotti Uses

  • Eating a Bispotti meets the Arocknid's Trick requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating a Bispotti helps to meet the Robean's Romance requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating a Bispotti helps to meet the Sour Profitamole's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 2 Bispotti helps to meet the Parmadillo's Romance requirements. (Pocket Paradise)

  • Bispottis dislike eating honey.
