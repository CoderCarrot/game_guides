Ancient Egyptians worshipped the God Khepri, who was associated with the beetle. On Piñata Island, the Sweetle is just another bug, so don't let it convince you to lend it money. Despite what it says, it does not have worshippers who will pay you back next Thursday.

The Sweetle is a beetle piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is one of the first few catchable piñatas in the Dessert Desert region. The Sweetle can be caught using a Trap.

Appearance

The Sweetle is a brown beetle piñata with a round body. Its irises are blue. It has a flat, shovel-like nose and six small legs that are patterned with orange and yellow rings. Its body is also patterned with orange, and it has a yellow circle on its forehead that looks like a sun.

Requirements (Trouble In Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Appears when you first visit the Dessert Desert.

Bait Requirements

  • Sweetles have a yearning for yams.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 1 square pinometer of sand in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Turnip.
  • Have 1 square pinometer of sand in the garden.
  • Have a Sweetle house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Has eaten 1 jar of Marmalade.

Variant 2 Requirements (White)

  • Has eaten 1 Snowdrop flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten 1 Water Lily Seed.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it an Apple Seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Carrot to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Ears
  • Pincer-like mouth
  • Horns around its mouth

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • You are a level 0 gardener or better.

Sweetle Uses

  • Eating 1 Sweetle helps to meet the Tartridge's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Sweetle helps to meet the Profitamole's Romance requirements.
