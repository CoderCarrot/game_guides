Pengum

Pengums left the jungles of Piñata Island to avoid a lifetime of servitude. The bigger, meaner Piñatas forced them to work as waiters. When they found the Piñata Island ice fields, they thought they'd found a safe new home. Unfortunately, they were penniless, so they set up a cafe where they could... er, serve the other Piñatas.

The Pengum is a blue penguin piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is only found in the Piñarctic.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You must be a level 2 gardener or better.
  • Can only be found in the Piñarctic.

Bait Requirements

  • A Pengum likes to eat something warming.
Note: Bait the trap with a Chili.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 5 square pinometers of snow.
  • You have 5 square pinometers of water.
  • Has eaten 1 Newtgat.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing Conga's Top Hat.
  • Is wearing a Toff Monocle.
  • Is wearing a Secret Agent Bowtie.
  • You have 5 square pinometers of snow.
  • Have a Pengum house in the garden.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a poppy changes its color to red.

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a turnip changes its color to purple.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it corn changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a snowdrop flower to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a tulip to have it perform trick 2.
    1/2

Wildcard Variants

  • Whiskers at the base of the beak
  • Mohawk
  • Yellow feathers on the flippers

Appear Requirements

Pengum Uses

  • Eating 1 Pengum helps meet the Sour Cocoadile Resident requirements.
  • Eating 3 Pengums helps meet the Polollybear Romance requirements.

Trivia
