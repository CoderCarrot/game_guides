Sherbat

When Piñatas were just evolving, what would become the Sherbat was developing a taste for extreme sports. For a time, it looked like street-pizza extinction. Then one morning, that strange creature with the sagging armpits became a Sherbat legend! It can bust some moves, too. I hear Dastardos appreciates a bit of Sherbat dancing.

Level

4

Base Value

1000 coins

Housing

Sherbat Home

Animal

Named after the Bat but has physical traits from the Megabat

Candy

Sherbet

Attack Type

For the sour variant of the Sherbat, see Sour Sherbat. For a list of all sour species, see the Sour Species category page.

The Sherbat is a dark blue bat piñata.

No Piñata would be too happy about having a Sour Sherbat (シャーバット Shābatto) attached to its neck. So dig out a Jack 'o Lantern and tame one quickly, then put your new Mothdrop-loving pet to work protecting all those sick Piñatas instead!

Appearance

The Sherbat has more something of a boxy Megabat than a bat like the Red Sour Variant. It has two ears with lightblue stripes, it has two really small wings as arms. It has black eyes with a light blue ring

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Sherbat resident in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 Mothdrop residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Mothdrops.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten a Tulip flower.
  • There is a Sherbat house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Cyan)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Sunflower Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a Chili Seed.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Sherbat resident in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 Mothdrop residents in the garden.

Resident requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Mothdrops
  • There is a Jack 'o' Lantern in the garden.

Romance requirements

  • Has eaten a Tulip flower.
  • There is a Sherbat Home in the garden

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a Chili Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

Variant 3 Requirements (Cyan)

  • Webbed ears

  • Attract it by having a Tulip in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have a Sherbat resident in the garden.
  • Nighttime in the garden.

Visit

  • Have 3 Mothdrop residents in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 2 Mothdrops.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Tulip flower.
  • Have a Sherbat house in the garden.

Variant (Red)

  • Has eaten a Chili.

Sherbat Uses

  • Directing a Sherbat to Dastardos will momentarily distract him, earning the player an achievement.
  • 5 Sherbats in the garden helps to meet the Hootyfruity's Visit Requirements.
  • Eating 2 Sherbats helps to meet the Hootyfruity's Resident Requirements.

Other information

  • Sherbats dislike being watered with the watering can.
  • Making a Sherbat wear a pair of Headphones greatly increases its happiness.
