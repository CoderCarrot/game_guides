Tiger Lily

This plant tries to protect its flower by making it look like a huge, snarling, ferocious Piñata. No, I completely agree, it doesn't really succeed.

Tiger Lily is an orange flower introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

Surface Requirements

  • Needs to be planted in soil, long grass or short grass

Fertiliser

  • 3 applications of orange fertiliser immediately after planting

Uses

  • 10 tiger lilies in the Garden helps meet the Tigermisu Appear requirement
  • 12 tiger lilies in the Garden helps meet the Tigermisu visit requirements
  • Feeding 1 Tiger lily to a Chocstrich changes it's colour to orange
  • Feeding 1 Tiger lily to a Lemmoning changes it's colour to orange

Trivia

  • It is one of the flowers in Viva Pinata that resembles its real counterpart the least.
  • Despite attracting the Tigermisu, it is poisonous to felids.
