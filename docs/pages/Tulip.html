Tulip

Many years ago, a man was washed up on the shore of Piñata Island. He was pulled from the sea by a family of hamsters. When he recovered, he could only remember his named (Dan) and in his pockets he had some seeds. The seeds grew into tulips, and the story was passed down. Now almost everyone knows about the tulips from Hamster Dan.
