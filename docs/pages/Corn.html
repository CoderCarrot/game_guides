Corn

Corn has wholesome yellow grains that some Piñata find irresistible. Who doesn't like lovely, sweet, sun-ripened corn? To some the humble ear of corn is the most controversial of all the vegetable plants and must be strenuously tested to make sure it does not cause offense! It sounds crazy but it's true!

Type

Vegetable

Level

2

Base Value

30 coins

Fertilizer

Yellow
Corn is a yellow vegetable that grows from a Corn Seed. Lottie will buy it from you for 30 Chocolate Coins. To increase its value, use yellow fertilizer on it.
