Thistle

The Thistle grows quickly and will spread to any 'spare' patch of earth. Not only will it soon fill the garden, it's covered with prickles that can make any piñata's life a misery. I'd suggest that you get rid of them all.

Type

Weed

Level

2

Base Value

3 coins

Weed Effects

  • Shocks piñatas, triggering them to fight
The Thistle is a weed plant. A thistle is used for romance, variant, and resident requirements. It causes Piñata to fight by shocking them with electricity if they come too close.

Appearance

The Thistle appears as a red, stick like plant. Its stem has black stripes on it, and several spikes protrude from it. It has a green, bulb shaped flower head that has lots of pink dots on it.

Acquisition

You may be able to achieve this seed from Costolot's Store after she reopens.

You can even earn the seed earlier, without purchase, if you whack Seedos enough times, and he will then leave thistles in your garden (original game only).

Purposes

  • Used to evolve the White Flutterscotch to a Purple Flutterscotch.
  • Can be used to make the Fudgehog visit.
  • Used to make the Fudgehog resident.
  • Used to change the Doenuts variant (along with Gooseberry Fool).
  • Used to make the Shellybean resident.
  • Used to make the Shellybean visit.
  • Can start fights between piñata.
  • Used to make the Mothdrop romance.
  • Used to change the Sparrowmint variant-purple

Trivia

  • It is the only truly useful weed, as it is important earlygame for a variety of Pinata, like Fudgehogs and Shellybeans.
