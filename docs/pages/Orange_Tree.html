An Orange tree is a tree plant introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise that grows oranges.

Appearance

The Orange Tree is a thin whire tree with thinner branches. Its leaves are green. Two light tan stripes on its slightly darker tan wood can bee seen.

Surface Requirements

  • Must be planted in soil or sand.

Fertilizer

  • 3 applications of Orange fertilizer. The first when the large buds first appear before they burst. The second when the large buds appear for a second time before they burst. The final when large buds appear for the third time.
