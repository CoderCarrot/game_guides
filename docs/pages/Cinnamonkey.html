Cinnamonkey

Everyone loves Cinnamonkeys with their cheeky faces and mischievous tricks. The more Cinnamonkeys the better, and the very best thing is to take a barrel and quite literally stuff it with the little tricksters. Great.

Level

7

Base Value

2800 coins

Housing

Cinnamonkey Home

Animal

Monkey

Candy

Cinnamon

Attack Type

Banana skins
A Cinnamonkey is a blue, red, and cyan monkey piñata. It has green irises. Its chin is red with gray-cyan square swirl patterns near its mouth and its nose, and there is a red stripe vertically across its face, with blue everywhere else along its face, except for its long ears which are cyan. Its arms are mostly blue with two red stripes across both arms. Its body is mostly blue, and its lower body is cyan making it seem like it is wearing cyan underwear. Its long red tail has blue stripes over it, and the blue stripes further away the body of the Cinnamonkey are thinner than the ones closer to its body, and its tail ends in a cyan ball.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Monkeynut Tree in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • There is a fully grown Monkeynut Tree in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • Have 3 fully grown trees in the garden
  • Has eaten 8 monkeynuts

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing a fez.
  • Has eaten 4 bananas.
  • Have a Cinnamonkey house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a Chili changes its color to red.

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a Blackberry Seed changes its color to purple.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily flower changes its color to pink.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • There is a monkeynut tree in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • There is a fully-grown banana tree in the garden.
  • Have 1 barrel in the garden.
  • Have 5 monkeynuts in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 bananas.
  • Has eaten 4 monkeynuts.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing a fez.
  • Has eaten 4 oranges.
  • There is a Cinnamonkey house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a chili changes its color to red.

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a blackberry Seed changes its color to purple.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily flower changes its color to pink.

Wildcard Variants

  • Very large ears
  • Large cylinder replacing the usual tail ball
  • Tail splits into three ends

Trick Requirements

  • Feeding it a Banana Split allows it to perform Trick 1.
  • Feeding it a Jar of Peanut Butter allows it to perform Trick 2.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having an Orange in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have a Monkeynut Tree in the garden.

Visit

  • Have a Monkeynut Tree in the garden.

Resident

  • Have 3 Monkeynut Trees in the garden.
  • Has eaten 8 Monkeynuts.

Romance

  • Has eaten 4 bananas.
  • Have a Cinnamonkey house in the garden.

Variant (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Blackberry Seed.

Cinnamonkey Uses

  • Eating 2 Cinnamonkeys helps to meet the Eaglair's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating a Cinnamonkey allows a Fizzlybear to perform Trick 2.

Species Conflicts

  • Roario (Avoids garden if resident)

Rivals

Other Information

  • Cinnamonkeys like being watered with the watering can.
