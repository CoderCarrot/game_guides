Custacean

Where you find a beach, you'll find these little Piñatas. They tend to be a bit grumpy, because those claws are not ideal for evenly spreading sun lotion, and they almost always get sunburned.

Level

4

Base Value

1000 coins

Housing

Custacean Home

Animal

Crab (Crustacean is the scientific group crabs are classified in)

Candy

Custard

Attack Type

Sea Mines
The Custacean is a red and yellow crab piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It has yellow eyestalks with brown irises. It has large yellow and brown claws.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You have 2 square pinometers of sand.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 10 square pinometers of sand.
  • You have 10 square pinometers of water.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 12 square pinometers of sand.
  • You have 12 square pinometers of water.
  • Have a surf board in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 apples.
  • Has eaten 1 watercress.
  • Have a Custacean house in the garden.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (White and Pink)

  • Feeding it an Orchid flower changes its color to white and pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a mushroom changes its color to orange.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a blackberry jam changes its color to purple.
1/2

Wildcard Variants

  • Spikes on back
  • Spikes on claws
  • Eyelashes

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Water Lily Seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Water Lily Flower to have it perform trick 2.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having 10 square pinometers (1%) of sand in the garden.

Custacean Uses

  • Eating 1 Custacean helps meet the Walrusk's Resident requirements
  • Eating 1 Custacean helps meet the Quackberry's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 2 Custaceans helps meet the Fizzlybear's Romance requirements.
