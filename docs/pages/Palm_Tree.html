Palm Tree

This tree makes any garden a tropical paradise. Although called a palm tree, it doesn't grow palms, so don't go waiting for the palm harvest. For safety reasons, you should wear a hard hat when under the branches.

Type

Tree

Level

9

Base Value

450 coins

Fertilizer

Brown
A Palm tree is a tree introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise that grows coconuts.

Appearance

A Palm tree appears as a thick tree with no branches.

Fertilization

Fertilize with Brown Fertilizer for the first time when the green bud appears at the top of the tree; After the second buds bursts, and sometime either at or around the third or fourth (and final) burst of buds.
