Twingersnap

When it comes to improving something, it's obvious that two is twice as good as one. This is the next step in Syrupent evolution. But they aren't just two heads - the left head is touch sensitive too!

Level

Animal

Gingersnap

Attack Type

Bottles of poison
The Twingersnap is a green two headed snake piñata. Most of its body are green, but the two heads are orange with different patterns on them.

Educated mistreatment of Syrupent eggs can result in this two-pronged mutation. It's still relatively easy to please, but will attract trouble from Sour Bonboons just like the lesser and greater members of its family. Similar mistreatment of the Twingersnap's eggs will result in a four headed mutation.

Requirements (Original)

To create a Twingersnap, romance any two Syrupents and then watch the resulting egg carefully. Eventually, it will bounce three times in quick succession, and on the third, largest bounce, you must smack it with your Shovel. Performing this correctly will cause the egg to hatch into a Twingersnap. Be careful, because hitting the egg too early will break it!

Evolution Requirements

  • After romancing any two Twingersnaps, simply perform the same steps that you did to obtain the Twingersnap in order to hatch a Fourheads from the resulting egg.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Squazzil.
  • Has eaten 2 Poppy Seeds.
  • Have a Twingersnap house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Snapdragon Seed.

Variant 2 Reqiurements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Venus Piñata Trap.

Variant 3 Reqiurements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Smash a Syrupent egg with your shovel right before it hatches to create a Twingersnap.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

To create a Twingernap, romance 2 Syrupents together and tap the resulting egg with the handle of your Shovel until a green mist emits from it. Once the egg hatches, it will be a Twingersnap.

Evolution

  • After romancing any two Twingersnaps, simply perform the same steps that you did to obtain the Twingersnap in order to hatch a Fourheads from the resulting egg.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 variant Squazzil.
  • Has eaten 2 Poppy Seeds.
  • Have a Twingersnap house in the garden.

Variant (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Venus Piñata Trap flower.

Twingersnap Uses

  • Having Twingersnaps in the garden helps to meet the Macaraccoon's Visit requirements.
  • Losing a fight to a Twingersnap helps to meet the Sour Bonboon's resident requirements.

In the TV Series

Tina and Teddington Twingersnap

Tina and Teddington Twingersnap are main characters of the Viva Piñata TV series. Tina and Teddington are opposites attached to the same body. Tina is the red-eyed female of the group and loves adventures and being tough. She is the more down-to-earth lowbrow of the two. Teddington is a blue-blooded wannabe and considers himself a member of the upper crust elite.
