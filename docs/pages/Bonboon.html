For the sour variant of the Bonboon, see Sour Bonboon. For a list of all sour species, see the Sour Species category page.

The Bonboon is a purple and red baboon piñata. It has a blue belly and cheeks, with white accents on both. Its mouth is red.

Quite unlike their sour counterparts, Bonboons can actually stop fights in your garden by performing a dance to distract angry piñatas.

This reformed Sour Piñata still loves to monkey around the garden. It's also one of the highest level piñatas you'll see, so get ready for a sugar rush!

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a resident Bonboon in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Monkeynut Tree in the garden.
  • Have 1 Banana Tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 7 bananas.
  • Has eaten 7 monkeynuts.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Bonboon house in the garden.
  • Has eaten 3 Jameleons.

Variant 1 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it an Orchid will change its color to white.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell will change its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Watercress Seed and a Bird of Paradise Seed changes it to yellow.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a resident Bonboon in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Monkeynut Tree in the garden.
  • Have 1 Banana Tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 7 Bananas.
  • Has eaten 7 Monkeynuts.
  • Have 1 Banana Tree grown in the garden.
  • Have 1 Monkey Nut Tree grown in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Bonboon house in the garden.
  • Has eaten 3 Jameleons.

Variant 1 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it an orchid flower changes its color to white.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell flower changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange and Green)

  • Feeding it a Bird of Paradise Seed changes its color to orange and green.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a Fire weed Seed.
  • Has eaten a Fire weed flower.

Wildcard Variants

  • A nose (like a Proboscis Monkey)
  • Top of head curved downwards (like a Angler Fish)
  • A red tail(?)

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Cinnamonkey resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 10 gardener or better.
  • Have a Bonboon resident in the garden.

Visit

  • Have 1 Monkeynut Tree in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 7 Bananas.
  • Has eaten 7 Monkeynuts.
  • Have 1 fully-grown Banana Tree in the garden.
  • Have 1 fully-grown Monkeynut tree in the garden.

Romance

  • Has eaten 3 Jameleons.
  • Have a Bonboon house in the garden.

Variant (White)

  • Has eaten an Orchid flower.

Bonboon Uses

  • A Bonboon can stop fights between other piñata by dancing to distract them.
  • 1 Bonboon in the garden helps to meet the Sarsgorilla's Appear Requirements.
  • 1 baby Bonboon in the garden helps to meet the Sarsgorilla's Visit Requirements.
  • Eating 1 Bonboon helps to meet the Roario's romance requirements if the piñata costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over. (Note: Roario needs to eat a single piñata which costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance.) (Another note: Bonboon's base value is naturally 3,600 chocolate coins by number. To make the Bonboon's base value up to 4,500 chocolate coins or over, you need to add more 900 chocolate coins or over to fit the Roario's romance requirement.)
