The Badgesicle is a purple badger Piñata. These crusty old fellows don't look so friendly, but don't be fooled - they're only as sweet as any other Piñata on the inside. Maybe that's because they use those sizeable snouts to scope out only the sweetest treats to eat!

An in-game rumor from Leafos claims that when a Badgesicle swims in water, it will turn into a Sweetooth. This is false.

Appearance

It has a long, pointed snout, with a round black nose on the end. It has a bold white line down the middle of its head, violet eyelids, and amber irises. It has a white underside, yellow ears, and a pointy little tail with white on the bottom. It has two yellow stripes and yellow markings down each side.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 2 nocturnal residents in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Garden has 4 nocturnal residents.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Newtgat.
  • Has eaten 2 Pumpkins

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Squazzil.
  • Has eaten 2 Tulip seeds.
  • Have a Badgesicle house in your garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it an Apple changes its color to red.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell flowerhead changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a jar of Honey and a Water Lily flowerhead changes its color to pink.

Happiness Tips

  • Badgesicles like eating Mothdrops, Sweetles, Whirlms, Bunnycombs, and honey.
  • Badgesicles dislike eating carrot cake, toffee apples, jam, muffins, and banana splits.
  • Badgesicles hate eating coconut macaroons.

In the TV Series

Beverly Badgesicle

Beverly Badgesicle is a major character of the Viva Piñata TV series. She is a big fan of Hudson Horstachio and is very obsessed with him.
