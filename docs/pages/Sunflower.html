The Sunflower is a flower plant. It is very tall and looks somewhat like the sun, hence the name Sunflower. It can be purchased from Costolot's General Store, or obtained from Seedos.

Appearance

Sunflowers are flowers with tall stems, which increase in height when fertilized. The flower head has a brown core surrounded with lots of yellow petals. Sunflower heads have a dark brown core, however, in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise when it is attached to a flower, it has a green core.

Surface requirements

  • Plant needs to be planted in grass, tall grass, or dirt.

How best to grow it

Use 3 applications of yellow fertilizer immediately after planting it, for maximum effect.

A fully catalyzed sunflower can be sold for 400 chocolate coins.

Uses

Sunflower seed uses

  • In TIP, feeding a Squazzil 3 sunflowers will help it reach itss Romance requirements.

Sunflower uses

  • If given to a Moozipan and direct it to a Milking Shed, you will get Milk.
