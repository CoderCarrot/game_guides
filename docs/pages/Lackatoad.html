Appearance

The Lackatoad is a purple deformed version of the Lickatoad.

Requirements (Original)

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding a Lickatoad a nightshade berry and then tapping it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 4 Arocknids in the garden.
  • You have 6 square pinometers of water (6%).
  • Has eaten 2 toadstools.
  • Have a Lackatoad house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell flower changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a banana tree seed changes its color to yellow.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding a Lickatoad a nightshade berry and then tapping it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 hemlock flowers.
  • Have 6 pinometers of water.
  • Has eaten 2 toadstools.
  • Have a Lackatoad house in the garden.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell flower changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a banana tree seed changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feeding it a nightshade berry seed will have it perform Trick 1
  • Feeding it a nightshade berry will have it perform Trick 2

Wildcard Variants

  • Antenna on head topped with a ball
  • Webbed feet
  • Webbed ears

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Feeding a Lickatoad a nightshade berry and then tapping it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Resident

  • Feeding a Lickatoad a nightshade berry and then smacking it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Romance

  • Have 4 Arocknids in the garden.
  • Have 60 pinometers of water.
  • Has eaten 2 Toadstools.
  • Have a Lackatoad house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower.

Lackatoad Uses

  • Eating 1 Lackatoad helps to meet the Fourheads's Romance requirements.

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten by

  • Bonboon
  • Fourheads

Scared of

  • Bonboon
  • Fourheads
  • Roario

Rivals

  • Salamango

Other information

  • Lackatoads can jump over fences.
  • Lackatoads can get sick if they're near a Monkeynut Tree.
  • Likes to be watered with the Watering Can.
