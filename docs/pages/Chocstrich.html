Chocstrich

The Chocstrich's unusual body shape is rumored to be a result of its stinky feet. The nose of the Chocstrich is at the furthest point away from the offending area.

The Chocstrich is a multicolored ostrich piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is the evolved form of Cluckles.

TiP Game Guide Description: Although the Chocstrich is a popular pinata at parties, it might make an even better impression if it didn't spend most of its time with its head stuck in the ground!

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding 2 Cactus seeds to a Cluckles turns it into a Chocstrich (earning the player an achievement).

Bait Requirements

  • A Chocstrich loves a tasty yam.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 2 square pinometers of sand.
  • Has eaten 3 cactus seeds.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 S'morepions.
  • Has eaten 3 Raisants.
  • Have 15 square pinometers of sand.
  • Have a Chocstrich house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a tiger lily flower changes its color to orange.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a blueberry muffin changes its color to blue.

Wildcard Variants

  • A ring of feathers around the top and bottom of neck.
  • Very large tail feathers.
  • Mohawk.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a cactus seed to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a cactus flower to have it perform Trick 2.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by feeding a Cluckles 2 Cactus seeds.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 7 gardener or better.

Visit

  • There are 5 Raisants in the garden.
  • Have 4 sandwiches in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 3 Raisants.
  • Has eaten 2 sandwiches.

Romance

  • Has eaten any 5 pieces of fruit.
  • Has eaten 1 Red Flutterscotch.
  • Have a Chocstrich house in the garden.

Variant (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Turnip Seed.

Chocstrich Uses

  • Eating a Chocstrich egg helps to meet the Pieena's Romance requirements.

Rivals

Happiness tips

  • Despite needing a yam to bait it into a trap and being stated that they love it in the bait requisites, Chocstriches upon becoming residents actually hate eating yams

In the TV Series

Despite not being able to fly in the game series, and being based off a flightless bird, the ostrich, a Chocstrich is shown flying in the episode The Transparent Trap to eat on Fergy Fudgehog's candy remains.

Corina Chocstrich

Corina Chocstrich is a character in the Viva Piñata TV series. She is the high school girlfriend of Hudson Horstachio. She is a skilled fighter.

Cherish Chocstrich

Cherish Chocstrich is a character in the Viva Piñata TV series. She doesn't like going to parties, the Cannoñata, and candy, believing that they are all evil.
