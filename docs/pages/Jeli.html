Appearance

The Jeli is a plump humanoid Piñata. It has two long arms which are about the length of its body. Its legs are short stumps below its torso. Its body has an alternating pattern between a light blue and pink, with its arms, feet, and head being pink. It has a dark blue nose and a similarly colored trim around the eyes.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 31 gardener or better.
  • Can only be found in the Piñarctic.

Bait Requirements

  • Bait trap with a holly berry.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 40 square pinometers of snow.
  • Has eaten 3 banana splits.
  • Has eaten 2 orchid seeds.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Figgy Puddings.
  • Have any 5 Piñarctic residents in the garden.
  • Have 50 square pinometers of snow.
  • Is wearing Snow Shoes.
  • Have a Jeli house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a sunflower changes its color to yellow.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a muffin changes its color to blue.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a Banana
  • Has eaten an Orchid

Wildcard Variants

  • Droopy ears
  • Spiky Mohawk
  • Big horns

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Jelis cannot be attracted in Just for Fun mode, except you can use the Post Office.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Appears midnight the first night you hit level 6. If not present, your save file did not trip the flag that spawns the Jeli and he will never appear.

Resident

  • Capture it? (The piñata must be hit with a shovel to meet this requirement.)

Romance

  • Have 500 pinometers of long grass (50%).
  • Has eaten 10 Rainbow Gems.
  • Have a Jeli house in the garden.

Variant (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Banana Split.

Jeli Uses

  • Eating 1 Jeli helps to meet the Roario's romance requirements.

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten by

  • Roario
  • Tigermisu

Scared of

  • Roario (Avoids or escapes garden if resident)
  • Tigermisu (Avoids or escapes garden if resident)

Rivals

  • None

Happiness Tips

  • The Jeli likes figgy pudding, and bananas.
  • The Jeli dislikes all seeds, holly berries, banana splits, and being watered.

Trivia

  • The existence of the Jeli species has been questioned since the PinataIsland.info episode guide was updated on September 23, 2006 with a description of an episode called "The Abominable Jeli". The episode first aired on May 20, 2007, confirming its existence, and on or before 13 May 2008, Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise was announced, where it was confirmed to be in the upcoming game.
