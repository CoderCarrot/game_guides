The Pigxie is a purple deformed pig Piñata with wings. It is the result of cross-romancing a Rashberry and a Swanana. It can fly unlike the Rashberry, and it is seemingly based on the phrase "When pigs fly".



Its body shape is very similar to the Rashberry. Its face is asymmetrical, with its head permanently twisted to the left. It has a mask-like pattern on the face.

Requirements (Original)

The Pigxie is created by cross romancing a Swanana and a Rashberry after buying a Mystery Home from Willy Builder. Therefore, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 bullrush flowers.
  • Has eaten 2 acorns.
  • Is wearing Tap Shoes.
  • The garden is worth 10,000 chocolate coins.
  • Have a Pigxie house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a gem seed, a water lily flower, and a poppy flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a gooseberry fool.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

The Pigxie is created by cross romancing a Swanana and a Rashberry after buying a Mystery Home from Willy Builder and have eaten Joy Candy. Therefore, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 bullrush flowers.
  • Has eaten 2 acorns.
  • Is wearing Tap Shoes.
  • The garden is worth 10,000 chocolate coins.
  • Have a Pigxie house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a poppy flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a gooseberry fool.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a rotten chili to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a rotten gooseberry to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Mangled tusks
  • Long ears
  • Facial feathers

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Pigxie is created by cross romancing a Swanana and a Rashberry after buying a Mystery Home from Willy Builder and meeting each of their individual breeding requirements. That includes buying a Swanana Home, Rashberry Home, Swan Fountain, having 300 pinometers of water (30%), and feeding them the correct items. You must also be a Master Romancer of both species. The Pigxie has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 2 Bullrush flowers.
  • Has eaten 2 Acorns.
  • The garden is worth 10,000 chocolate coins.
  • Is wearing Tap Shoes.

Variant (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Fool.

Pigxie Uses

  • Eating 1 Pigxie helps to meet the Sour Mallowolf's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Pigxie helps to meet the Roario's Romance requirements, if the piñata costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over (Note: a Roario needs to eat a piñata which costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance.) (Another note: The Pigxie's base value is naturally 3,600 chocolate coins by number. To make the Pigxie's base value up to 4,500 chocolate coins or over, you need to add 900 more chocolate coins or over to fit the Roario's romance requirement)
  • Eating 1 Pigxie helps to meet the Dragonache's Trick 2 requirements.

Glitches and Errors

  • For a flying pig-based piñata, the Pigxie is surprisingly slow. This can lead to the 'Romancing Problem' notification showing up if you romance them far away from the Pigxie House. Ignore this notification - They have no problems entering the home, it's just that they haven't arrived at the house's entrance yet!

Other information

  • While it's easy to romance a Rashberry, it is often hard to romance Swananas. If you direct one piñata to the other and the Swanana game comes up, try directing the other piñata to the first. Then you will probably get the Rashberry game.
  • Pigxies enjoy being watered with the Watering Can.
  • Most baby Piñatas are scared of Pigxies.

Commonly Asked Questions

My Swanana and Rashberry won't romance. What do I do?

There are a few reasons why this might not happen. Make sure you've met their romance requirements and that you've built a Pigxie Home (listed as a Mystery Home in Willy Builder's) as well as houses for both Rashberry and Swanana. Feed each a piece of Joy candy, so they're more inclined to cooperate. If you've directed the Swanana to the Rashberry and they shake their heads, try directing the Rashberry to the Swanana.

What level do you have to be to get the Mystery Home?

In Viva Piñata and Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise, you only need to have had both a Swanana and a Rashberry as residents at some point to unlock the Mystery Home. As soon as you have these piñata unlocked and have attracted them to live in your garden (or bought a Rashberry from Paper Pets if playing on the original) you can purchase a Mystery Home.

In Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, you need to have romanced each species normally (with another piñata of the same kind) at least once before the Mystery Home will be available for sale. The Rashberry unlocks far earlier than the Swanana does in this game, so you'll be waiting until the Swanana's Appear requirements are met at level 8 to be able to get a Pigxie.
