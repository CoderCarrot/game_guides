The Horstachio is a blue and and purple horse piñata. It has a blue head, with purple markings, green irises, and a light lilac muzzle, and blaze markings. Its mane and tail are various shades of orange and yellow. The Horstachio has piebald blue and turquoise markings on its body, and violet feathering on its lower legs.

The Ponocky is a stepping stone towards obtaining the Horstachio. The Horstachio can be evolved into a Zumbug.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a blackberry and a daisy to a Horstachio turns it into a Zumbug.

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Ponocky in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have a Ponocky in the garden.
  • Have 500 square pinometers of grass (50%).

Resident Requirements

  • Have 500 square pinometers of grass (50%).
  • Has eaten 8 apples.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 500 square pinometers of grass (50%).
  • Is wearing a Rosette.
  • Has eaten 1 toffee apple.
  • Has eaten 3 carrots.
  • Have a Horstachio house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it watercress changes its color to green.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a chili seed changes its color to red.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a blackberry and a daisy to a Horstachio turns it into a Zumbug.

Appear Requirements

  • Have a hay bale in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 50 square pinometers of Grass in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 50 square pinometers of Grass in the garden.
  • Has eaten four apples.
  • Has eaten four carrots.

Romance Requirements

  • Have a Horstachio house in the garden.
  • Have 50 square pinometers of Grass in the garden.
  • Has eaten a Toffee Apple.
  • Has eaten a Horseradish.
  • Is wearing a Rosette.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it Watercress changes its color to green.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a Chili Seed changes its color to red.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Daisy seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Carrot cake to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Thick tail instead of tassels
  • Mandibles
  • Two body-colored horns from sides of jaw

Glitches and Errors

  • In the original game, the Horstachio house's entrance has a very narrow opening that just barely allows a Horstachio to face forward and enter the house. This can lead to romancing problems where two Horstachios are trying to enter at the same time but neither can since they're both pushing each other away from the narrow entrance.

Other Information

  • Likes eating apples, carrots, and horse radishes.
  • Dislikes eating bones, cheese, Jack 'o' lanterns, sandwiches, and stew.
  • Hates eating coconuts, fir cones, gems, hazelnuts, and monkeynuts.

In the TV Series

Horstachio characters usually stand up like a biped creature, unlike in the game series where they always walk on four legs.

Hudson Horstachio

Hudson Horstachio is one of the main characters of the Viva Piñata TV series. He is a celebrity and wants to be the spotlight in most events. As Hudson is a main character in the TV series, he has appeared in many episodes.

Hamilton Horstachio

Hamilton Horstachio is a character in the Viva Piñata TV series. Featured in the episode Horstachio of a Different Color he is Hudson Horstachio's archrival. In the episode he managed to become more popular than Hudson making Hudson depressed. Hamilton enters a Horstachio competition and gets disqualified due to him transforming into a Zumbug.
