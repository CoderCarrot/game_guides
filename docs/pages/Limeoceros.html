Limeoceros

When Piñata Central was built, they needed a way to transport Piñata around the island. Limeoceri love to dance an Irish Jig, so they brought in off-road trucks, equipped with Irish folk music and loudspeakers. As the Limeoceri followed the music, they hammered new roads over Piñata Island. The Limeoceros is still helpful - see what happens when Professor Pester comes a calling.

Limeoceros is a green, purple and burgundy rhino Piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It also appears in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise.

The Limeoceros can ram Professor Pester out of the garden.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have had a Limeoceros resident at some point.

Bait Requirements (Dessert Desert)

  • Any bait will do

Visit Requirements

  • Have 5 Thistles in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 fire weed seed.
  • Has eaten 1 thistle seed.
  • Has eaten 1 prickly pear seed.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing a Fruity Hat.
  • Is wearing Bling Earrings.
  • Has eaten 2 coconuts.
  • Have 40 square pinometers of sand in the garden.
  • Have a Limoceros house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Cactus flower changes its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeing it a piece of Cheese changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell changes its color to blue.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Venus Piñata Trap seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Venus Piñata Trap to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard #1: Triceratops frill
  • Wildcard #2: Two horns
  • Wildcard #3: Long horn

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 1 yam in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 yam in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 1 yam in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Have a Limoceros house in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • Have 100 pinometers of long grass (10%).
  • You are a level 8 gardener or better

Visit

  • Have 250 pinometers of long grass (25%).
  • There are 5 orchids in the garden.
  • There are 5 chilis in the garden.
  • There are 5 tulips in the garden.

Resident

  • Have 400 pinometers of long grass (40%).
  • Has eaten 5 orchids.
  • Has eaten 5 chilis.
  • Has eaten 5 tulips.

Romance

  • Have 500 pinometers of long grass (50%).
  • Has eaten 4 turnips.
  • Have a Bispotti resident in the garden.
  • You have the Bispotti Master Romancer Award.
  • Have a Limeoceros house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a jar of jam.

Limeoceros Uses

  • Can chase Professor Pester out of the garden. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Having 3 Limeoceroses in the garden helps to meet the Tigermisu's Visit requirements. (Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Limeoceros helps to meet the Tigermisu's Resident requirements. (Pocket Paradise)

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten by

  • Roario
  • Tigermisu

Scared of

  • Roario (Avoids garden if resident)
  • Tigermisu (Avoids garden if resident)

Rivals

  • None

Other Information

  • Likes eating coconuts and chilis.
  • The Limeoceros is the only tamed Sour Piñata to have an appear cutscene.
  • Professor Pester will not mark on a Limeoceros resident, like the Mallowolf, but he will suck in its life candy and other piñata candy, if broken by another object such as the shovel, despite that Limeoceros will charge at Professor Pester and will eject him out of the garden, if resident.
