Syrupent

The only thing the Syrupent has in common with the less reputable members of its family is its shape. "What kind of snake is that" you may say? The best kind.

Level

2

Base Value

300 coins

Housing

Syrupent Home

Animal

Serpent (or simply Snake)

Candy

Syrup

Attack Type

Dice
The Syrupent is a green snake piñata. Its irises are purple and its lips are colored a brighter shade of green than its head.

The first rung on the ladder leading to piñatas with double and quadruple the fun. The Syrupent itself needs little more than grass and Mousemallows (and maybe a ban on Macaraccoon) to keep it content.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • After romancing 2 Syrupents together, watch the resulting egg carefully. Eventually, it will bounce three times in quick succession, and on the third, largest bounce, you must smack it with your Shovel. Performing this correctly will cause the egg to hatch into a Twingersnap. Be careful, because hitting the egg too early will break it!

Appear Requirements

  • Have 10 square pinometers of grass (1%).

Visit Requirements

  • Have 10 square pinometers of grass (1%).

Resident Requirements

  • Have 20 square pinometers of grass (2%).
  • Has eaten a Mousemallow.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Lickatoad.
  • You have 30 square pinometers of grass (3%).
  • There is a Syrupent house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Buttercup flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • After romancing 2 Syrupents together, watch the resulting egg carefully. Eventually, it will bounce three times in quick succession, and on the third, largest bounce, you must smack it with your Shovel. Performing this correctly will cause the egg to hatch into a Twingersnap. Be careful, because hitting the egg too early will break it!

Appear Requirements

  • You have 1 Square Pinometer Of Grass

Visit Requirements

  • You have 1 Square Pinometer Of Grass and have 2 Mousemallows in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • You have 2 Square Pinometers Of Grass
  • Has eaten a Mousemallow.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Robean
  • You have 3 Square Pinometers Of Grass
  • There is a Syrupent Home in the garden

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Buttercup flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Turnip Seed to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Turnip to have it perform Trick 2.

  • Wildcard 2: Frill on top of head
  • Wildcard 3: Ball at end of tail

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having 10 square pinometers (1%) of grass in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Evolution

  • After romancing 2 Syrupents together, tap the resulting egg with the handle of your Shovel until a green mist emits from the egg. Once the egg hatches, it will be a Twingersnap.

Appear

  • You have 10 pinometers of grass (1%).

Visit

  • You have 10 pinometers of grass (1%).

Resident

  • You have 50 pinometers of grass (5%).
  • Has eaten a Mousemallow.

Romance

  • You have 100 pinometers of grass (10%).
  • Has eaten 1 Lickatoad.
  • Have a Syrupent house in the garden.

Variant (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Buttercup flower.

Syrupent Uses

  • 5 Syrupent Family Residents in the garden helps to meet the Macaraccoon's Visit requirements.
  • Defeating 3 Syrupents in a fight helps to meet the Macaraccoon's Resident requirements.
  • Syrupents can be evolved into Twingersnaps and sold for a profit.
  • Losing a fight to a Syrupent helps to meet the Sour Bonboon's resident requirements.
