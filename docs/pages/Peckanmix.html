Other birds may declare themselves to be the 'most beautiful creature in the world' (yes Swanana, I'm talking about you), but the Peckanmix has the class to really carry it off. I'm sure this amazing looking Piñata will ruffle some feathers.

Appearance

Its irises are blue and it has a yellow beak, with a blue tuft of feathers on top of its head. Its neck is cyan with blue stripes. Its belly is a cyan-green color, and it has orange wings. It has a multicolored train, or waft of feathers, on its back.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Swanana resident in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 peas in the garden.
  • Have a birdbath in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 peas.
  • Has eaten 1 Blue Flutterscotch.
  • Has eaten 1 Green Flutterscotch.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 gem.
  • Has eaten 1 bowl of pea soup.
  • Is wearing a Tiara of Tranquility.
  • Have a Peckanmix house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it wool changes its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a gooseberry fool changes its color to green.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a pumpkin seed changes its color to orange.

Wildcard Variants

  • Three Large feathers on the top of head
  • Five small feathers on the back of head
  • Three large discs on front of neck

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Blueberry to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it Watercress to have it perform Trick 2.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a pea in the garden.

Peckanmix Uses

  • Eating 2 Peckanmixes helps to meet the Tigermisu's Romance requirements.

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • Blue Flutterscotch
  • Green Flutterscotch

Eaten by

  • Tigermisu
  • Pieena

Scared of

  • Pieena
  • Pretztail (Avoids Gardens if Resident)
  • Roario
  • Tigermisu (Avoids Gardens if Resident)

Rivals

  • Swanana

Happiness Tips

  • Peckanmixes loves eating peas.
  • Peckanmixes dislikes eating gooseberries and apples.
  • They hate eating yellow, purple, and red gems.
