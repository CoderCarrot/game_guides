Appearance

The Goobaa is a piñata that changes state depending on its happiness (candiosity), first appearing as a thin black piñata that gains its notoriously round wooly state at a high enough happiness (candiosity) level.

Requirements (Original)

The Goobaa cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 2310 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 16. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 water lily.
  • Has eaten 2 poison ivy.
  • Have 120 square pinometers of long grass (12%).
  • Have a Goobaa house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Black)

  • Feeding it a tulip seed changes its color to black.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • 5 Snowdrops in the garden.
  • Have 4 square pinometers of long grass.

Unblock Requirements

  • Have 3 Candaries in the garden to wake the sleeping Chippopotamus.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 6 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Have 8 Snowdrops in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 8 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Eat 3 Snowdrops.
  • Have 12 fences in garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Eat 1 thistle.
  • Eat 2 poison ivy flower.
  • Have 12 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Have a Goobaa house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell flower changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Black)

  • Feeding it a Tulip seed changes its color to black.

Trick Requirements

  • Daisy for trick 2.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Goobaa cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 2310 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 3. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Water Lily flower.
  • Has eaten 1 Poison Ivy flower.
  • Have 120 pinometers of long grass (12%).
  • Have a Goobaa house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower head.

Goobaa Uses

  • Eating 1 Goobaa helps to meet the Mallowolf's Romance requirements.
  • A Goobaa can produce Wool by directing it to a Shearing Shed. After the Goobaa has been sheared, you will need to feed it a Sunflower in the original, a Watercress in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, or refill its Candiosity in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise before it will be ready to produce again. The Bonnet accessory (purchased from Paper Pets or Gretchen Fetchem's) causes it to produce Wool automatically.

Rumors

  • An in-game rumor from Leafos claims that a Goobaa will not romance without its fleece. (False)

Glitches and Errors

  • In the original Viva Piñata, there is a graphical glitch which causes variant Goobaas to appear to be a different color when viewed from afar.
  • In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise, seeing the variant 3 requirements, the text was supposed to tell you to feed it a water lily seed, but it says to feed it a flower instead.
