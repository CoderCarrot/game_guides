Licorice
The Lickatoad is a green frog Piñata.

It has a lighter green/yellow marker on its upper face, and it has brown irises. Its tongue is purple. It has a pattern covering its body in various shades of green, it also has small green forelimbs, and longer hind legs that are usually in an arched position.

Some species like to remain inconspicuous, but the Lickatoad is a prominent presence in the garden as it hustles Newtgats and tries to survive in a world of predatory Syrupents and Crowlas. Life's never dull.

Langston Lickatoad is a talking piñata who manages the Cannoñata.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding it a Nightshade Berry and then hitting it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Appear Requirements

  • You have the pond head for your shovel.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 1 square pinometers of water (1%).

Resident Requirements

  • You have 2 square pinometers of water (2%).
  • Has eaten 1 Taffly.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Green Flutterscotch.
  • Have a Lickatoad house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell and a Gooseberry changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a Carrot and an Apple changes its color to red.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to purple.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Evolution

  • Feeding it a Nightshade Berry and then hitting it with the shovel before it gets sick will evolve it into a Lackatoad.

Appear

  • You are a level 4 gardener or better.

Visit

  • Have 10 pinometers of water (10%).

Resident

  • Have 20 pinometers of water (20%).
  • Has eaten 1 variant Taffly.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Green Flutterscotch.
  • Have a Lickatoad house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Lickatoad Uses

  • Having 2 Lickatoads in the garden helps to meet the Crowla's Visit requirements.
  • Eating a Lickatoad helps to meet the Syrupent's Romance requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 2 Lickatoads helps to meet the Crowla's Resident requirements.

Other Information

In the TV Series

Langston Lickatoad is a character in the TV Series.

He operates the Piñata cannon at Piñata Central and manages Candiosity. If a piñata has too little candy in it, they are rejected.

Trivia

  • Grunty from Banjo-Kazooie is transformed to a pumpkin from Viva Piñata during the intro of Banjo-Kazooie: Nuts & Bolts right before Banjo and Kazooie are similarly transformed into a Lickatoad, but the proportions of the legs are slightly off.
