The Arocknid is a Piñata species introduced in the original Viva Piñata. It is a green spider with orange stripes across its abdomen as well as purple stripes across its legs and neck. Arocknids have four red eyes and diamond patterns all along their body.

This eight-legged Piñata is perfect for any birthday party that takes place on October 31, but it's not always the most popular Piñata on the block. There's a reason some folks can't wait to whack it with a stick, and it's not just the candy inside!

Appearance

The Arocknid is a green spider-like Piñata. Its body is striped with orange, and it has eight small, stick like legs that are striped with purple. It has four eyes, two on either side of its head. Its eyelids are purple and its irises are red. On the front of its upper jaw, above its mouth, it has two protrusions that seem to resemble fangs.

Arocknid Uses

  • Having 4 Arocknid residents in the garden helps to meet the Lackatoad's Romance requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Having 3 Arocknid residents in the garden helps to meet the Profitamole's Visit requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Having 4 Arocknid residents in the garden helps to meet the Profitamole's Visit requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 2 Arocknids helps to meet the Profitamole's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating an Arocknid helps to meet the Profitamole's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating an Arocknid helps to meet the Macaraccoon's Romance requirements.
  • Having 2 Arocknid residents in the garden helps to meet the Parmadillo's Visit requirements. (Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating an Arocknid helps to meet the Parmadillo's Resident requirements. (Pocket Paradise)

Happiness Tips

  • Arocknids dislike eating Snapdragons, Tulips, Water Lilies, Bullrushes, and Watercresses.
  • Arocknids hate eating Orchids.

Other Information

  • Arocknids dislike being watered by the watering can and will react startled when the watering can is held near them.

In the TV Series

Artie Arocknid

Artie Arocknid is the caretaker of Piñata Island's Tutie Fruity Jellybean watering hole. He is irritated by Monty Mousemallow's antics.
