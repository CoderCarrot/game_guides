The Profitamole is a yellow mole piñata.

A harmless ambling bundle of fluff, if you can banish that rampaging Sour side. Profitamoles tend to enjoy Arocknids for dinner, light mine clearance work and being able to actually see their romancing partners. Profitamoles have strange, slit like eyes, that look best when you give it a pair of Bottle's Glasses (from Banjo-Kazooie).

The Profitamole might have the tiniest eyes of any Piñata. Of course, when you spend most of your life underground, there's not much to look at!

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have one resident Profitamole in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 4 Arocknid residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Arocknids.
  • Has eaten 1 Whirlm.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing Bottles' Glasses.
  • Has eaten 3 carrots.
  • Have a Profitamole house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Watercress flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Nightshade Seed and a Bluebell Seed.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Tame a Sour Profitamole and make it a resident.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 Arocknid residents in the garden.
  • Have 3 Whirlm residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Arocknid.
  • Has eaten 1 Whirlm.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Profitamole house in the garden.
  • Is wearing a pair of Bottles' Glasses.
  • Has eaten 1 Sweetle.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Watercress flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Nightshade Seed.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a thistle seed.
  • Has eaten a thistle flower.

Profitamole Uses

  • Profitamoles will eat the patches of dirt left by a Mine, Mine Workings. (Original and Trouble in Paradise)
  • 5 Profitamoles in the garden helps to meet the Pudgeon's Unblock requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • A Profitamole may be sent into a Mine to search for the Dragonache Egg. (Pocket Paradise)

In the TV Series

Prewitt Profitamole

Prewitt Profitamole is one of the major characters of the Viva Piñata TV series. He is a repair mechanic and repairs broken piñatas.

Pierce Profitamole

Pierce Profitamole is one of the major characters of the Viva Piñata TV series. He was a Sour Profitamole that was transformed into a regular Profitamole after eating Toadstools. He originally acted as a voice for Fergy Fudgehog's Rock Candy band, but after turning into a normal Profitamole he forgot how to do the voice lines for the band and Franklin Fizzlybear had to take over.
