Appearance

The Fourheads is a green hydra Piñata with three heads and a fourth head on the end of its tail. One of its heads has a red highlight, another one has an orange mark, another head is green, and the head used as its tail is a slightly orange green head.

Requirements (Original)

To create a Fourheads, romance any two Twingersnaps and then watch the resulting egg carefully, with your shovel ready. If you hit it too early, you'll break it. It will bounce repeatedly for while, with small hops. As soon as it makes a set of 3 bigger, more animated bounces, hit it with your shovel to reveal a baby Fourheads.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Lackatoad.
  • Has eaten 2 nightshade berries.
  • Has eaten 1 toadstool.
  • Have a Fourheads house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a jar of honey changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine changes its color to pink.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

To create a Fourheads, romance any two Twingersnaps and then watch the resulting egg carefully, with your shovel ready. If you hit it too early, you'll break it. It will bounce repeatedly for while, with small hops. As soon as it makes a set of 3 bigger, more animated bounces, hit it with your shovel to reveal a baby Fourheads.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Lackatoad.
  • Has eaten 1 S'morepion.
  • Has eaten 1 hemlock flower.
  • Have a Fourheads house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a jar of honey changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine changes its color to pink.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it stew to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Mousemallow to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Horns and fins on head
  • Whiskers on side of jaws
  • Large frill behind heads

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

To create a Fourheads, romance any two Twingersnaps and then simply tap the egg with the handle of your shovel until green mist comes out. When it hatches, you'll have a Fourheads. There is no danger of damaging the egg if your timing is off.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 variant Lackatoad.
  • Has eaten 2 Nightshade Berries.
  • Has eaten 1 Toadstool.
  • Have a Fourheads house in the garden.

Variant (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Fool.

Fourheads Uses

  • Having Fourheads as residents helps to meet the Macaraccoon's visit requirements.
  • Losing a fight to a Fourheads helps to meet the Sour Bonboon's resident requirements.
  • Eating 1 Fourheads helps to meet the Roario's romance requirements, if the piñata costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over. (Note: Roario needs to eat a single piñata which costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance.) (Another note: The Fourheads' base value is naturally 3,600 chocolate coins by number. To make the Fourheads' base value up to 4,500 chocolate coins or over, you need to add 900 more chocolate coins or over to fit the Roario's romance requirement.)
  • Fourheads are useful to give to Piñata Central when asked to give a high value piñata.
