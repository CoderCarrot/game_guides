The Salamango is a red scorching-hot salamander piñata. It is the evolved form of the Newtgat.

Switching elements as it evolves, the moist Newtgat becomes the scorching Salamango - so hot it lives in a volcano and swallows Reddhotts. Unfortunately, its predecessor's grudges evolve too...

Requirements (Original)

Resident Requirements

  • Feed a Newtgat a Chili to evolve it into a Salamango.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Reddhott.
  • Has eaten 2 Chili Seeds.
  • Has a Salamango house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Banana.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pale Blue)

  • Has eaten a bottle of Milk and a Nightshade Seed.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Reddhott.
  • Has eaten 2 Chili Seeds.
  • Has eaten 2 Fireweed Seeds.
  • Have a Salamango house in the garden.

Salamango Uses

  • Having 5 Salamangos in the garden helps to meet the Elephanilla's Unblock requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Salamango helps to meet the Roario's Romance requirements, if the piñata costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over. (Note: a Roario needs to eat a single piñata which costs 4,500 coins or over to romance.) (Another note: The Salamango's base value is naturally 3,600 chocolate coins by number. To make the Salamango's base value up to 4,500 chocolate coins or over, you need to add more 900 chocolate coins or over to fit the Roario's romance requirement.)
  • Eating 1 Salamango helps to meet the Dragonache's Mature requirements.

Other Information

  • Salamangos hate to be watered.
  • Salamangos like eating all seeds.
  • Because Salamangos are level 8 piñatas and are relatively easy to obtain from newtgats, breeding their wildcard variants is one of the best early game methods of making chocolate coins.
  • With the Salamango being one of the slowest Piñata in the game, you may encounter a 'Romancing Problem' Notification if you romance them far from their home. Additionally, if you romance them very far from their home, they could become heartbroken before reaching their home, even if they'd normally have no difficulties entering the home
