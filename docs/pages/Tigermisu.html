Tigermisu

This Piñata is so chilled it never even changes out of these stripy pajamas. Just because it looks like an oversized tabby cat doesn't mean you can forget about treating it with respect. I've heard stories about hunters armed with nothing more than a bowl of warm milk and a bow on a string. All they ever find is the hunters' hats lightly sprinkled with salt and pepper.

Level

10

Base Value

5500 coins

Housing

Tigermisu Home

Abilities

  • Can eat all pinatas level 9 or lower.
  • Can scare off any non-resident pinata, except for Roario.

Animal

Bengal Tiger

Candy

Tiramisu
Tigermisu is an orange tiger piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It also appears in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise.

This fierce feline is new to the Piñata scene, but it has already staked out its territory - and it has some problems with Roario declaring itself king of the garden!

Appearance

It has a white "beard" on its cheeks, a brown "mask" surrounding its eyes, and its muzzle and ears are yellow. Its paws and the tip of its tail are white, and it has black stripe patterns on its orange body.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 8 Tiger Lilies in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 12 Tiger Lilies in the garden.
  • Have a Zumbug resident in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Moojoos.
  • Has eaten 1 Zumbug.
  • Has eaten 1 Buzzlegum.

Romance Requirements

  • Is wearing a Sabreman Helmet.
  • Has eaten 1 Camello.
  • Has eaten 2 Peckanmix.
  • Have a Tigermisu house in the garden.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a Hoghurt.
  • Has eaten a Flapyak.

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Banana Split.

Variant 2 Requirements (White)

  • Has eaten a Horse Radish.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Wildcard Variants:

  • Saber-toothed
  • Fluffy tail tip
  • Mane

Requirements (Just for Fun)

You cannot get Tigermisu in Just for Fun mode.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 10 gardener or higher.

Visit

  • Have 3 Limeoceroses resident in the garden.
  • Have 3 Elephanillas resident in the garden.

Resident

  • Has eaten 1 Limeoceros.
  • Has eaten 1 Elephanilla.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Chippopotamus.
  • Has eaten 1 Eaglair.
  • Have a Tigermisu house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bone.
