The Hoghurt is a yellow warthog piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is the evolved form of the Rashberry.

Its irises are pink and it has a red ring around its eyes. It has long ears which end in red tips. It has two white, short, curly tusks near its square red nose.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a Cheesecake to a Rashberry turns it into a Hoghurt, earning the player an achievement.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Muffin.
  • Is wearing Big Jolly Lips.
  • Is wearing a Weather Girl Wig.
  • Is wearing Pendant Earrings.
  • Have a Hoghurt house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Buttercup changes its color to yellow.

Variant 2 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a Tulip changes its color to purple.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Carrot changes its color to pink.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Prickly Pear seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Fireweed to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Tusks curl inward
  • Ball on tail
  • Mohawk along body

Hoghurt Uses

  • Eating 3 Hoghurts helps to meet the Sour Mallowolf's Resident requirements.
  • Feeding a Hoghurt to a Tigermisu will teach it Trick 1.
