For the sour variant of the Mallowolf, see Sour Mallowolf. For a list of all sour species, see the Sour Species category page.

The Mallowolf is a blue wolf piñata, despite being called a gray wolf in reality.

It is particularly unhelpful in its Sour form as it scares off visitors, and pigs must fly before it is tamed. However, manage that and you'll earn yourself a loyal Mallowolf pet and defender against Ruffians. After being tamed its eyes start blinking red and it is able to scare Ruffians.

Appearance

It has a periwinkle colored head with a long, royal blue snout, and a navy blue nose on the end, it has dark blue eyelids, and brown irises. Its nose is much longer than its mouth. It has a white and periwinkle zig-zag pattern around its neck, and cornflower-blue ears and body. Its hind legs and rump are royal blue, and its tail is periwinkle and cornflower-blue, with a white tip. The Mallowolf also has visible teeth on its upper jaw.

Mallowolf Uses

  • Having 1 Mallowolf in the garden helps to meet the Mallowolf's Appear requirements.
  • A Ruffian can be scared out of the garden by directing a Mallowolf to it. Doing this for the first time will earn the player an Achievement.
  • 2 Mallowolves are required to meet the Parrybo's unblock requirement (Trouble in Paradise).
