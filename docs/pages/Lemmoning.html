Lemmoning

The Lemmoning has no sense of danger. For example, if there were a boulder balanced precariously on a stack of cream buns, we would see the danger and avoid the whole thing. A Lemmoning would feel hungry, grab the nearest bun and tuck in. Lemmonings can process sweeties by converting one kind into another - you'll need to practice to find out how that works exactly.

The Lemmoning is a yellow lemming piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It can convert Sour Candy into Happy Candy by eating one. It doesn't get sick from this, either.

Appearance

The Lemmoning appears as a round lemon-shaped piñata. Its head is yellow and its body is purple and green.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a resident Lemmoning in the garden.

Bait Requirements

  • A Lemmoning loves a really tasty sunflower seed.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 4 square pinometers of snow.
  • Has eaten 2 radishes.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 fir cones.
  • Have 4 square pinometers of snow.
  • Have a Lemmoning house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a Pea pod changes its color to green.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Tiger Lily changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Thistle seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Thistle to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard 1: Tail feathers
  • Wildcard 2: Feathery ears
  • Wildcard 3: Feathery whiskers

Lemmoning Uses

  • Eating 1 Lemmoning helps to meet the Kittyfloss romance requirements.
  • Eating 2 Lemmonings helps to meet the Badgesicle and Hootyfruity romance requirements.
