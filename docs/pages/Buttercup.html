Buttercup

Do you like butter? Grow this little yellow flower and then put your chin over it. If your chin is yellow, you like butter. If your chin is another color, you should probably visit a doctor.

Appearance

The Buttercup is a plant with short green stems. At the top lies a yellow flower head.

Terrain

  • Grass
  • Long Grass
  • Soil

Uses

  • Eating 1 Buttercup seed is needed to make a Mousemallow want to romance.

Bonus Growth

Use Yellow fertilizer 3 times immediately after planting.

Extra use

If given to a Buzzlegum, you can direct it to a Honey Hive to get Honey

If the flower head is given to a Sparrowmint, it will evolve into a Candary
