S'morepion

If you have these Piñatas in your garden, make sure you check your boots before starting work. S'morepions love the cozy confines of footwear to nap in, and they will sting you if you threaten to crush them with your feet. Better still, they like to keep in practice with their stings - and a Ruffian is a good target.

Appearance

It is a small yellow-green scorpion piñata with blue, green, and pink markings across its body. It has a tail with a spike on it. It has three eyes above a small set of mouthparts.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a S'morepion resident in the garden.

Bait Requirements

  • Any bait will do.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 5 square pinometers of sand (5%).
  • Has eaten 2 Venus Pinata Trap flowers.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Nightshade berries.
  • Have 5 square pinometers of sand (5%).
  • Have a S'morepion house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (White)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Milk.

Variant 2 Requirements (Orange)

  • Has eaten a Jack 'o' Lantern.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten a Cactus flower.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a hemlock flower to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a toadstool to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Curled stinger
  • Longer pincers
  • Four extra stingers

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • You are a level 0 gardener or better.

S'morepion Uses

  • A tamed S'morepion can sting Ruffians and chase them out of your garden.
