Poison Ivy

Poison Ivy is a good name - it tells you that this plant is a bit like ivy but most importantly it's poisonous! Don't let your Piñatas taste it, and keep it away from your other plants, or it will make a space for itself. Nasty, nasty nasty.

Poison Ivy is a poisonous weed plant. It costs 60 Chocolate Coins to sell to Lottie's Costolot's Store. Poison ivy is a weed that can cause other piñatas to become sick.
