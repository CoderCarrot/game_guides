Venus Piñata Trap

This plant seems to have taken a stand for plants. It's fed up with the 'plant grows - animal eats plant' order of things and has decided to dish out some spiny plant revenge. Keep it away from your Piñatas.

The Venus Piñata Trap is a type of weed plant. An evil plant, almost any piñata that eats it will be sick. The only piñatas that enjoy it are the Eaglair, Twingersnap (also adds a variant), and Parrybo (also adds a variant)

In Viva Piñata, it is useful for increasing wealth. If fenced off it can be a good source of coins, the head sells for 360 and it generally produces more than one seed. However, in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise weed heads sell for negative coins, meaning that Lottie will charge for selling these items.
