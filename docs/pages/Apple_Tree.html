Apple Tree

Lots of creatures like to eat apples, and this is the most efficient way to get them fresh. This tree may be small, easy to grow and cheap to get hold of, but it is worth its weight in apples!

The Apple Tree is a tree plant that can be grown from Apple Seeds. Once fully grown, it produces Apples. Its seed can be purchased from Costolot's Store in the village, costing 55 Chocolate Coins in Viva Piñata and 7 Chocolate Coins in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

Appearance

The Apple Tree is a tree with eight branches and green leaves. A sun marking is seen on the wood of the tree.

Growth requirements

  • Tree needs to be planted in grass, soil, or long grass.
  • Use red fertilizer or direct a Cocoadile to the plant for bonus growth, allowing for extra apples to grow on the tree.

Uses (Original)

  • 1 fruit tree of any kind in the garden helps meet the Raisant Visit requirements.
