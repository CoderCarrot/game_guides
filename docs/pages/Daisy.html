Daisy

Type

Flower

Fertilizer

Yellow
A Daisy is a yellow flower.

The white stripe on the side there is probably the best hint at the colour of what will grow from this seed.

Daisy seed grows a daisy.

A humble little daisy is easy to grow, but it still looks pretty. It's surprisingly popular for such a common flower.

The base value of a daisy is 10 coins, however a fully catalysed daisy can be sold for 40 coins.

Requirements

Surface requirements: Daisy seed should be planted in grass, soil or long grass.

Catalyst requirements: For bonus growth Cocoadile tears, yellow fertilizer or special mix fertilizer should be used.

Uses

Appear

  • 1 daisy in the garden is required for a White Flutterscotch to appear.

Visit

  • 2 daisies in the garden is required for a White Flutterscotch to visit.

Resident

  • 4 daisies in the garden is required for a White Flutterscotch to become a resident.
  • Eating 1 daisy and 1 buttercup is required for the Bunnycomb to become a resident.

Romance

  • Eating 1 daisy seed is required for a Mousemallow to romance.
  • 12 daisies in the garden is required for a Moozipan to romance.

Evolve

  • Eating a daisy and a blackberry is required for a Horstachio to evolve into a Zumbug.
