Polollybear

Many years ago, a group of Fizzlybears became fascinated with playing hide and seek. The games got more and more extreme until the whole group had moved up to the snow covered parts of Piñata Island and turned white. Now they are almost invisible most of the time. Technically, they are 'winning' the hide and seek game, but there aren't as many Polollybears as there used to be.

The Polollybear is a light blue polar bear piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is the evolved form of the Fizzlybear, and can be found in the Piñarctic after a player has evolved a Fizzlybear into a Polollybear.

The Polollybear is boxy and has darker blue markings over its body. Its head is long and boxy as well.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding a blue gem to a Fizzlybear changes it into a Polollybear, earning the player an Achievement.

Bait Requirements

  • A Polollybear likes a tasty snowdrop flower.

Resident Requirements (Wild)

  • You have 20 square pinometer of snow.
  • Has eaten 3 blue gems.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Pengums.
  • Has eaten 2 pots of honey.
  • Have 25 square pinomitors of snow.
  • Have a Polollybear house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a dandelion.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a water lily.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a gooseberry.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a ball of Wool to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Fir Cone to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Large diamond shaped tail
  • Ridges above ears
  • Larger lower jaw

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)
