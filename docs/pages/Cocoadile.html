For the sour variant of the Cocoadile, see Sour Cocoadile. For a list of all sour species, see the Sour Species category page.

The Cocoadile is a green crocodile piñata. Its irises are orange. Its mouth is incredibly long, much bigger and longer than its back, with white teeth jutting out even when its mouth is fully closed. It has red markings on its head, back, and lower body. Its lower body is a slightly lighter shade of green. Its small legs are placed close to each other on its back, leaving its large mouth to stand up with little support from the legs.

The Cocoadile can be directed to fertilize plants using Cocoadile tears. This is required to unlock an Achievement in Viva Piñata.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have had a tame Cocoadile resident at some point.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 16 square pinometers of water (16%).
  • Have 6 Quackberry residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 19 square pinometers of water (19%).
  • Has eaten 3 Quackberries.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 22 square pinometers of water (22%).
  • Has eaten 2 Juicygeese.
  • Has Gold Teeth
  • Have a Cocoadile house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Light Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Buttercup Seed

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have had a tame Cocoadile resident at some point.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 16 square pinometers of water (16%).
  • Have 5 Quackberry residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 19 square pinometers of water (19%).
  • Has eaten 1 Quackberry.
  • Has eaten 1 Sweetooth.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 22 square pinometers of water (22%).
  • Has eaten 1 Swanana.
  • Has Gold Teeth
  • Have a Cocoadile house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell changes its color to light blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a buttercup seed changes its color to yellow.

Wildcard Variants

  • Ridges on back
  • Triangle tail tip
  • Ears above eyes

Trick Requirements

  • Feeding it a Prickly Pear Seed will allow it to perform Trick 1.
  • Feeding it a Thistle will allow it to perform Trick 2.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Sweetooth resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 8 gardener or better.
  • Have a Cocoadile resident in the garden.

Visit

  • Have 160 pinometers of water. (16%)
  • Have 5 Quackberry residents in the garden.

Resident

  • Have 190 pinometers of water. (19%)
  • Has eaten 3 Quackberries.

Romance

  • Have 220 pinometers of water. (22%)
  • Has eaten 2 Juicygeese.
  • Have a Cocoadile house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a bluebell flower.

Cocoadile Uses

  • If you direct a Cocoadile to a plant that can be fertilized, it will fertilize them with its tears, earning the player an achievement.
  • Eating one Cocoadile helps meet the Choclodocus's Mature requirements.

Glitches and Errors

  • Due to a misspelling, in the original Viva Piñata the journal says that 5 Quackberry residents are needed to meet the Cocoadile's visit requirements when you really need 6.

Other Information

Cocoadiles like to be watered with the watering can.

In the TV Series

In the TV series, Cocoadiles stand up in a stance that makes them look like they have a pot belly. Their bodies are also longer.

Cecil Cocoadile

Cecil Cocoadile is one of the major characters of the Viva Piñata TV series. His tears were used to water and fertilize Chortles Chippopotamus' garden.
