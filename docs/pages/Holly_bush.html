Holly bush

Holly bush

Amidst the dark green, waxy leaves grow some very tasty little red berries. Ideal for the smaller Pinatas that live in colder parts of the island.

Type

Bush

Level

2

Base Value

30 coins

Fertilizer

Red
The Holly bush is a bush plant introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

You can purchase holly seeds or holly berries from Costolot's Store, use your seed pouch, or try talking with Seedos to get a holly bush seed.

Appearance

Surface Terrain Requirements

It will only grow in snow or dirt.

Holly Bush Uses

  • 1 Holly Bush in the garden helps meet the Fudgehog appear and visit requirements

Holly Berry Uses

  • Eating 1 holly berry helps meet the Fudgehog residence requirement
  • Eating 1 holly berry helps meet the Red Flutterscotch romance requirements
  • 1 holly berry helps meet the Robean bait requirement
  • 1 holly berry meets the bait requirements for the Jeli
  • Holly Berries can be transformed into Figgy pudding

Holly Seed Uses

  • Eating 1 holly seed helps meet the bispotti and Squazzil trick requirements

Fertilising

3 applications of red fertiliser. The first immediately after planting, the second once the first set of buds appear and the third after the second set of buds has appeared.
