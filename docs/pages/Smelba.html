A Smelba is a small pink, green, and white skunk piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

A Smelba can be used to scare Ruffians away. Target it with the cursor and then select the Ruffian with the cursor. The Smelba will emit a dark green cloud and the Ruffian will be scared and leave the garden.

Appearance

The Smelba has four incredibly small legs tucked under the back half of its body. Those legs are hidden most of the time, as they easily sink into grass and are covered by the cursor. Its nose is dark pink. It has a large white stripe on its back and tail, with a green semicircle pattern on its lower body and tail. It has white eyelids, making it appear as if it has no irises when it sleeps. It also has small light pink ears, the green pattern continuing on the top of its head.

Happiness Tips

  • Smelbas love eating roses, cheese, garlic, air freshener and garlic butter.
  • Smelbas don't like eating snapdragon seeds, tulip seeds, sunflower seeds, watercress seeds, bluebell seeds, daises, dandelions or sunflowers.

Housing

The Smelba Home is a pink perfume bottle with a huge hole in the front.

Trivia

  • Smelbas are mentioned in the journal entry for the Gas Mask.
  • Smelbas and sour Smelbas their sour variant both dislike being watered with the watering can.
