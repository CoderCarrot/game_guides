Flapyak

This Piñata is a cousin to the Moozipan. Where the Moozipan enjoys gossip, the Flapyak prefers peace and quiet. Although the Flapyak may be considered to be more difficult to make resident than the Moozipan, you should find it's just as productive. Warm up the milking shed!

The Flapyak is a blue, red, yellow, and orange yak piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

It is able to produce Milk if fed a Joy Candy. After producing milk, its udders will be engorged. It can be milked at Milking Shed. If the Flapyak is accessorized with a Bell it will automatically produce milk by itself and automatically go into the Milking Shed. The Moozipan can produce milk in the same matter.

Appearance

It is heavily built with a bulky neck and torso. Its mouth is blue, and the rest of its head is yellow and red. Its horns are blue with yellow spots, long, and curves at the end. Its torso has a multi colored pattern of orange, and red. It has pink udders and teats, though the pink teats are significantly shorter than the Moozipan's teats, as it is nearly flush with its udders. It also has a short orange tail.

When it is ready to be milked, its udders and teats will start flashing white and be larger than normal. However, a glitch can occur where the udder is restored to normal size after an animation completes, but the udder will continue flashing even if the glitch happens.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 15 gardener or better.
  • Can only be found in the Piñarctic.

Bait Requirements

  • A juicy orange will attract a Flapyak across the ice.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 10 square pinometers of snow (1%).
  • Has eaten 4 fir cones.
  • Has eaten 1 figgy pudding.

Romance Requirements

  • Has drunk 1 pot of coffee.
  • Has eaten 3 gooseberries.
  • Is wearing a Daisy Hair Flower.
  • Have a Flapyak house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a blueberry changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Red)

  • Feeding it a chili changes its color to red.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a watercress flower changes its color to green.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it an orange tree seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it an orange to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • A 3rd horn in the middle of the head (pointing backwards)
  • Three tails
  • Hooked horns

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Moozipan resident reward.

Flapyak Uses

  • If fed a Joy Candy and directed to a Milking Shed, the Flapyak will produce Milk. If it wears a Bell, it will go to the Milking Shed on its own and automatically produce Milk.
  • Eating 1 Flapyak helps meet the Tigermisu trick requirements.

Other information

  • Despite their ability to walk on water, Flapyaks hate being watered with the Watering Can.

Housing

The Flapyak Home is a steel silver-colored submarine built into the ground that the Flapyak built themselves for a school project.

In the TV Series

Flapyaks in the TV series are generally shown with large muscular bodies.

Freddy Flapyak

Freddy Flapyak is a character in the TV series. He is a bulky wrestler for the Wide World of Roughhousing.
