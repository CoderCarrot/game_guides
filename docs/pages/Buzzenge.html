Buzzenge

Aloof and mean-looking, the Buzzenge will probably be the first bird of prey to visit your garden.

Level

6

Base Value

2100 coins

Housing

Buzzenge Home

Animal

Buzzard

Candy

Lozenge

Attack Type

Flamenco fans
The Buzzenge is a brown Buzzard piñata. It has a yellow beak, it has markings that make it look like it is wearing glasses, and it has blue irises. It has a big light brown and darker brown tail, large brown wings, and yellow feet. It also has various shades of brown in a pattern covering its body, belly, and tail.

Requirements

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Candary in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 5 Candaries in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have a Fir Tree in the garden.
  • Has eaten 3 Candaries.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten a Pudgeon.
  • Has eaten a Sparrowmint.
  • There is a Buzzenge house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine changes its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a buttercup changes its color to yellow.

Trouble in Paradise Requirements

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Candary in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 4 Candaries in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Sparrowmint.
  • Has eaten 2 Candaries.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Pudgeon.
  • Has eaten 1 Robean.
  • Has eaten 1 Tartridge.
  • There is a Buzzenge house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Bottle of Medicine will change its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed will change its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Buttercup changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Flutterscotch to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Taffly to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard 1: Larger tuft on the back of head
  • Wildcard 2: Longer and Sharper Beak
  • Wildcard 3: Large neck feathers

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Sparrowmint resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 5 gardener or better.
  • Have a Candary in the garden.

Visit

  • Have 5 Candaries in the garden.

Resident

  • Have a Fir Tree in the garden.
  • Has eaten 3 Candaries.

Romance

  • Has eaten 2 Pudgeons.
  • Has eaten 2 Sparrowmints.
  • Have a Buzzenge house in the garden.

Variant (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a buttercup flower.

Buzzenge Uses

  • Eating 4 Buzzenges helps to meet the Eaglair's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Buzzenge helps to meet the Vulchurro's Trick 2 requirements. (Trouble in Paradise only)

Glitches and Errors

  • Due to a misspelling, the original Viva Piñata's journal says that the Buzzenge needs to eat 2 Pudgeons and 2 Sparrowmints to romance when it only needs to eat 1 of each. In Pocket Paradise, the Buzzenge actually does need to eat 2 of each of these piñata.
