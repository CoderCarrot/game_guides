Appearance

It has purple splat markings around its mouth, purple eyelids, and blue irises. Its eyes protrude from its head. Its tail has purple stripes down the first half, while the second half is curled with purple spots. It has purple stripes down its body and legs, and it has three purple spines down its back.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 3 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 4 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 5 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Green Flutterscotch.
  • There is a Jameleon house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it a bottle of medicine and an orchid flower changes its color to white.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a piece of carrot cake changes its color to orange.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 3 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 4 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 5 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Taffly.
  • Have a Red Eye Rainbow in the garden.
  • There is a Jameleon house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it an orchid flower changes its color to white.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a piece of carrot cake changes its color to orange.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick 1: Feed it a Taffly.
  • Trick 2: Feed it a Buzzlegum.

Wildcard Variants

  • Three curls on tail
  • Small spines on head crest
  • Long ears

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Flutterscotch resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 4 gardener or better.
  • Have 3 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Visit

  • Have 4 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Resident

  • Have 5 differently colored Flutterscotches.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 Green Flutterscotch.
  • Have a Jameleon house in the garden.

Variant (Orange)

  • Has eaten a slice of Carrot Cake.

Jameleon Uses

  • Eating 3 Jameleons helps to meet the Bonboon's Romance requirements.

Eaten by

  • Bonboon (Romance Requirement and will not visit the garden if present.)

Rivals

Scared of

  • Bonboon
  • Eaglair

Other Information

It may take some work to get 5 Flutterscotches of different colors. Once you do it, though, you can keep them in the garden and sell any Jameleons that wander in for 1500 Chocolate Coins each. Alternatively, you can romance both the Jameleons and their food if you want to take things into your own hands, rather than waiting for Jameleons to visit.

  • Jameleons dislike being watered with the watering can.
  • Jameleons will not visit your garden if one or more Bonboons is a resident.
  • A Jameleon will turn invisible when it gets startled or is about to hunt its prey (TiP only).

Trivia

  • The Jameleon's romance dance is a mix tape of various Banjo-Kazooie and Grabbed by the Ghoulies tunes.
