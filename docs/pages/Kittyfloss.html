Kittyfloss

Sure, you can buy a Kittyfloss, but is it your pet or do you belong to it? Just when you think you've stamped authority on the situation, you'll find a "present" in your shoe.

Animal

Candy

Candy Floss

Attack Type

Fur Balls
The Kittyfloss is a domestic orange cat piñata. Its ears are orange and red, its eyelids are red, and it has orange irises. It has a long thin tail with yellow and red rings leading to an orange tip. The Kittyfloss has a yellow face with a red and orange pattern. It has an orange body, and red feet.

Piñata Island pet shops cater equally to Kittyfloss and Barkbark lovers, but don't try to raise both at once.

Requirements (Original)

The Kittyfloss cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 1650 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 10. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 ball of wool.
  • Has drunk 1 bottle of milk.
  • Has eaten 1 Mousemallow.
  • Have a Kittyfloss house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily flower changes Kittyfloss' color to purple.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell flower changes Kittyfloss' color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Bottle of Medicine changes Kittyfloss' color to hot pink.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 2 Mousemallows in the garden.
  • It is nighttime in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 3 Mousemallows in the garden.
  • Have a bottle of milk in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten a Mousemallow.
  • Has eaten a Sparrowmint.
  • Has drunk a bottle of milk.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten a Lemmoning.
  • Has eaten a Candary.
  • Have a Kittyfloss house in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Kittyfloss cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 1500 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 6. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 ball of Wool.
  • Has drunk 1 bottle of Milk.
  • Has eaten 1 Mousemallow.
  • Have a Kittyfloss house in the garden.

Variant (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Kittyfloss Uses

  • Having 1 Kittyfloss in the garden helps to meet the Barkbark's Appear requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Having 3 Kittyfloss in the garden helps to meet the Barkbark's Visit requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Kittyfloss helps to meet the Barkbark's Romance requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
