Parrybo

If Parrybos get bored, they use their clever mimicry to dial out for pizza. Parrybos don't even like melted cheese-they just want to see you arguing with the delivery boy for their twisted amusement.

The Parrybo is a rainbow parrot piñata. It's beak is yellow, it has three purple, and pink feathers on top of its head, its eyelids is blue, and it has chartreuse irises. Its head is red, its neck is green, it has a blue torso, and a purple belly. It has a yellow tail with pink tips, small red and green wings, and orange feet.

Friend to the grizzled sea cap'n but also a dedicated jungle, which seems to cause remarkably few issues. Tropical fruit is the key to Parrybo residence, but romancing is a whole other piratical kettle of fish. Being a parrot, this is not the kind of piñata for someone who gets headaches easily. Being loud vocally and visually, the Parrybo may seem like a bad piñata. But you would be mistaken by saying that. Its eye-catchingly vibrant colors will bring life to an otherwise dull garden.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 32 gardener or better.
  • Have a Bird of Paradise plant in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 2 Bird of paradise plants in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have a Banana Tree in the garden.
  • Has eaten 10 bananas.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Bird of Paradise seeds.
  • Has eaten 1 Orchid Seed.
  • Is wearing an eyepatch.
  • You have a Pirate Statue in the garden.
  • You have a Parrybo house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a Venus Piñata Trap changes its color to orange.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it an Oak Seed and a piece of Cheese changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell and a Nightshade Berry changes its color to blue.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a Bird of Paradise plant in the garden.

Unblock Requirements

  • Have enough Mallowolves to scare away the Ruffian.

Note: Meet the Appear requirements and have 2 Mallowolf residents.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 2 Bird of Paradise plants in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Bird of Paradise seeds.
  • Has eaten 2 Oranges.
  • Has eaten 2 Monkeynuts.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Banana Seeds.
  • Has eaten 1 Orchid Seed.
  • Is wearing an eyepatch.
  • Have a Pirate Statue in the garden.
  • Have a Parrybo house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a Venus Piñata Trap changes its color to green.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a piece of Cheese changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell changes its color to blue.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick #1: Eaten a Poppy Seed.
  • Trick #2: Eaten a Candary.

Wildcard Variants

  • Curly tail
  • Wooden leg
  • Large feather crest on head

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Blueberry in your garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 9 gardener or better.

Visit

  • Have 1 Bird of Paradise plant grown in the garden.

Resident

  • Have 1 Banana Tree grown in the garden.
  • Has eaten 10 Bananas.

Romance

  • Has eaten 3 Bird of Paradise seeds.
  • Has eaten 1 Orchid Seed.
  • Have a Pirate Statue in the garden.
  • Have a Parrybo house in the garden.
  • Is wearing an Eyepatch.

Variant (Orange)

  • Has eaten the flower from a Venus Piñata Trap.

Parrybo Uses

  • Eating 2 Parrybos helps to meet the Eaglair's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Parrybo helps to meet the Roario's Romance requirements, if the piñata costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over. (Note: a Roario needs to eat a piñata which worth 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance) (Another note: Parrybo's base value is naturally 3,600 chocolate coins by number. To make the Parrybo's base value up to 4,500 chocolate coins or over, you need to add 900 more chocolate or over to fit the Roario's romance requirement.)

In the TV Series

According to Teddington Twingersnap, Parrybo is pronounced pa-ree-bo.

Pierre Parrybo

Pierre Parrybo is a major character in the Viva Piñata TV series. Pierre is a feisty, fiery, fiercely French-accented Piñata. He is a minor celebrity on Piñata Island, and he is often asked to officiate at various contests, fairs, pageants, and similar things. He also acts as a host for events such as the Party Animals Championship Challenge.
