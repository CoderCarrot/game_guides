Tartridge

This little Piñata seems calm and sensible, but in a party situation it loses its head! After the events of the Piñata Flying Club Christmas party, the Tartridge lost its pilots license. Grounded, and all because of that trick with the egg whisk and a bar of soap. Still, at least the feathers seem to have grown back...

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 5 daisies in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 5 daisies in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 daisy seeds.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Sweetle.
  • Have a Tartridge house in the garden.
1/1

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Blueberry.

Variant 2 Requirements (Orange)

  • Has eaten an Orange Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Radish seed to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Radish to have it perform Trick 2.
1/2

Wildcard Variants

  • Sharp Beak
  • Tail Feathers
  • Extra head feathers

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Tartridges cannot be attracted in Just for Fun mode, except you can use the Post Office.

Tartridge Uses

  • Having 1 Tartridge in the garden helps to meet the Pretztail's Visit requirements.
  • Eating 1 Tartridge helps to meet the Pretztail's Resident requirements.
  • Eating 1 Tartridge helps to meet the Buzzenge's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Tartridge helps to meet the Twingersnap's Romance requirements.

Happiness Tips

  • Tartridge likes to eat Bread, Carrot Cake, Corn, Sweetles, and Daisy Seeds.
  • They dislike eating Pumpkins and Radishes
  • Tartridges dislike being watered by the watering can
