Here's a Piñata that just can't stop laughing. You can explain this by saying that it has a very positive outlook on life, but I suspect it's because the Pieena is one sandwich short of a picnic.

The Pieena is an orange hyena piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It is the evolved form of the Pretztail. After obtaining a Pieena via evolution, wild variants will begin to appear in the Dessert Desert.

We don't know what's so funny, but the Pieena always seems to be laughing its head off about something! Whatever it is, it probably isn’t exciting news.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a Pretztail a Bone evolves it into a Pieena.

Bait Requirements

  • A Pieena loves a juicy orange.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 2 pinometers of sand in the garden.
  • Has eaten a Bone.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten a Chocstrich egg.
  • Have 4 pinometers of sand in the garden.
  • Have a Pieena house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it an Orange changes its color to orange.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Blueberry changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (Purple)

  • Feeding it a Blackberry changes its color to purple.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick #1: Has eaten a Bone.
  • Trick #2: Has eaten a Jelly.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard #1: Large block-like ears with a notch taken out of them
  • Wildcard #2: Fangs
  • Wildcard #3: Shark Fin

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

Feeding a bone to a Pretztail evolves it into a Pieena

Romance Requirements

  • Have a Pieena house in the garden

Pieena Uses

  • Eating 1 Pieena helps to meet the Roario's Trick 1 requirements.
