Cactus

A cactus stores up all the 'good stuff' in the desert and then has to grow some wicked spines to prevent anything else stealing its resources. Life is very harsh out there in the sand.

Type

Flower

Level

8

Base Value

Fertilizer

The Cactus is a green flower introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

Appearance

The Cactus appears as a tall, green wood-looking plant. It has two branches on opposite sides of itself. Three pink flowerheads appear on the top of the three branches of the plant.

Surface Requirements

  • Must be planted in sand or soil

Fertiliser

  • 3 applications of green fertiliser immediately after planting

Uses

  • 1 cactus in the garden helps meet the Vulchurro Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 cactus helps meet the Chocstrich and Vulchurro Trick requirements.
  • Eating 3 cacti helps meet the Camello Resident requirements.
  • Feeding a cactus to a Geckie, Limeoceros and S'morepion changes its colour pink.
