Hazel Tree

If you want nuts, you should grow this little tree. It'll give you nuts and then some. When you have nuts, the Piñatas that like nuts will appear - they may even stay, just because you have nuts. Crazy!

Hazelnut trees are large trees that grow Hazelnuts. Once you obtain a Hazelnut Seed from Seedos, they can be purchased from Costolot's Store for 83 Chocolate Coins. You can find Hazelnuts in Costolot's Store under the 'Fruit' tab. Fully-grown, uncatalyzed Hazelnut trees are worth 150 chocolate coins.

Surface Requirements

Hazelnut Seeds can be planted in soil, grass, or long grass.

Catalysing (fertilizing)

Hazelnut trees require Cocoadile Tears, Brown Fertilizer, or Special Mix Fertilizer for bonus growth. Fertilizer should be added three times: First, immediately after planting, second, after the tree sheds its first set of leaves, and third, when the tree sheds its second, and last, set of leaves. A fully-catalyzed Hazelnut Tree is worth 630 chocolate coins.

Fruit

Hazelnut Trees produce Hazelnuts which are important for the following Piñata:

  • Squazzils need to eat 3 Hazelnuts before they can become Residents.
  • Rashberrys need to eat 1 rotten Hazelnut to meet their Romance Requirements.

Trivia

  • The Hazelnut items are inconsistently named. The tree that produces Hazelnuts is named the Hazel Tree, yet the seed is called Hazelnut Seed and the fruit it produces is called "Hazelnut" in the journal and when viewed in game.
