Ponocky

Clippety, cloppety. Little hooves tapping on the ground, the swish of a well-combed tail, and a gentle snort. What garden is complete without a Ponocky?

Level

6

Base Value

2100 coins

Housing

Ponocky Home

Animal

Pony

Candy

Pocky

Attack Type

Sugar lumps
The Ponocky, whose name is a portmanteau of pony and pocky, is a small yellow domestic pony piñata with a red tail. It has blue irises and long ears. It has an orange pattern on its whole body.

It is a stepping stone towards obtaining the Horstachio.

Every little girl wants a Ponocky at her birthday party. And, secretly, a lot of the grown-up ones do too!

Requirements (Original)

The Ponocky cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 2310 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 20. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten a piece of Carrot Cake.
  • Is wearing a Tail Bow
  • Have any 10 flowers in the garden.
  • Have 300 square pinometers of grass (30%).
  • Have a Ponocky house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it a piece of bread changes its color to white.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a bluebell seed changes its color to blue.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have 10 square pinometers of grass in the garden.
  • Have 10 carrots in the garden.
  • Have 10 flowers of any type in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have Bart tinker the obstructing metal railings into pieces of candy for 1,000 coins. If you have issues with tinkering it, restart your Xbox and then reload the game to fix it!
  • Have 15 square pinometers of grass in the garden.
  • Have 10 carrots in the garden.
  • Have 10 flowers of any type in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Have 20 square pinometers of grass in the garden.
  • Has eaten 5 Carrots.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Ponocky house in the garden.
  • Has eaten a Carrot Cake.
  • Is wearing a Tail Bow.
  • Have a Hay Bale in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (White)

  • Has eaten a loaf of Bread.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten an Apple.
  • Has eaten a Toffee Apple.

Wildcard Variants

  • Singular tail instead of tallows
  • Bunny ears
  • Large tail

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having 10 square pinometers (1%) of long grass in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Ponocky cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 2100 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 6. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Has eaten 1 piece of Carrot Cake.
  • Have any 10 flowers in the garden.
  • Have 300 pinometers of grass (30%).
  • Have a Ponocky house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Ponocky Uses

  • Having 1 Ponocky in the garden helps to meet the Horstachio's Appear requirements. (Original)
  • Having 1 Ponocky in the garden helps to meet the Horstachio's Visit requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten By

  • Roario

Rivals

  • Chewnicorn
  • Horstachio
  • Zumbug

Glitches

  • Sometimes Bart will not display the option to free the Ponocky. Exiting and re-entering the garden can fix this.
