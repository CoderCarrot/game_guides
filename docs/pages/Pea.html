Pea

Little green pods bursting with sweet peas inside. Delicious on their own or mashed up and made into soup.

Type

Vegetable

Level

5

Base Value

150 coins

Fertilizer

A pea is a green vegetable introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise.

Having 3 peas is one of the requirements for Peckanmix to appear in the garden.
