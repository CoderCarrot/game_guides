Robean

This cheeky little piñata adds a splash of color to the frozen wastes of Piñata Island. No matter how cold and bleak, the Robean is always cheery and full of bounce. How irritating.

The Robean is a brown ball-shaped robin piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It has a red and cyan pattern on its body.

It can only be found in the Piñarctic.

Appearance

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Can only be found in the Piñarctic.

Bait Requirements

  • A Robean can't stop eating holly berries.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 1 square pinometer of snow (1%).

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Bispotti.
  • Have 3 square pinometers of snow (3%).
  • Have a Robean house in the garden.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Fire weed flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry Fool.

Variant 3 Requirements (Orange)

  • Has eaten a Carrot.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a hazelnut seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a hazelnut to have it perform trick 2.
1/2

Wildcard Variants

  • Small Mohawk-like hair
  • Tail feathers
  • Feather whiskers

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • You are a level 0 gardener or better.

Robean Uses

  • Eating 1 Robean helps to meet the Syrupent's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Robean helps to meet the Buzzenge's Romance requirements.
