Shellybean

Now I'm all for security, but surely the Shellybean goes a step too far. It has a shell house on its back, and it needs another house to sleep in at night. Just imagine them all inside, not talking to each other, curled up in their individual shells. In the day, you can get the Shellybean to help out by eating weed seeds.

The Shellybean is a blue and orange snail piñata. It has orange eyestalks with blue stripes wrapping around them, and green irises. It has a yellow tail with a blue and orange pattern, and red tentacles protruding out of each side of its mouth. It has yellow markings above its mouth, and a multiple layered circular shell. The outer layer is blue, then orange, then blue again, while the apex of the shell is red.

The first Sour to visit and least difficult to tame, the Shellybean progresses from seed thief to tidy weed removal device once converted. In fact, thistles are a well-known Shellybean delicacy. Why ask questions?

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a resident Shellybean in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Thistle in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Thistle.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Bluebell Seed.
  • Have a Shellybean house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Gooseberry.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Has eaten an ear of Corn.

Romance Requirements

Other information

  • Shellybeans enjoy being watered with the Watering Can.
  • Although having a Shellybean around can be very useful for getting rid of unwanted weed seeds, be careful! Shellybeans do not need to be directed to weed seeds to eat them, and may end up eating seeds that you didn't want them to if you don't pay attention.
  • Shellybeans are very easily saddened and can leave your garden quickly, due to the fact that they are easily scared and trampled by bigger piñata due to being small and slow. You should pay attention to your Shellybean to check if they might leave your garden.

In the TV Series

Shirley Shellybean

Shirley Shellybean is a major character in the Viva Piñata TV series. She is the slowest piñata on Piñata Island.
