The Juicygoose is a purple goose piñata. Its beak is purple, and it has green irises. It has light lavender markings around its eyes, a pointy little tail, and purple wings with a yellow zig-zag pattern. Its body is mostly orange, with a thick pink band in the middle, and green markings dividing the colors. The Juicygoose also has purple feet.

While the Juicygoose is instinctively a sentry bird, its aggressiveness can result in tussles with the very Piñata it evolves from. It also frightens off Newtgats, making it tricky to indulge the Juicygoose's appetite. Juicygeese can be used instead of guard Barkbarks. This suggests that Juicygeese are belligerent, but in fact they just want to appear useful. Useful animals don't tend to end up roasted, with gravy... and mashed potatoes, yum!

Requirements (Original)

The Juicygoose does not have Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding 1 gooseberry to a Quackberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Romance Requirements

  • You have 100 square pinometers of water (10%).
  • Has eaten 2 Newtgats.
  • You have a Fish Fountain in the garden.
  • Have a Juicygoose house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it an oak tree seed changes its color to green.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink and Orange)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed, bird of paradise seed, and a poppy changes its color to pink and orange.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a banana changes its color to yellow.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Resident Requirements

  • Feeding 1 gooseberry to a Quackberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Arctic Juicygoose Bait Requirements

  • Any bait will do.

Arctic Juicygoose Resident Requirements

  • 8 square pinometers of snow.
  • Has eaten two gooseberries.

Romance Requirements

  • You have 10 square pinometers of water.
  • Has eaten 2 Newtgats.
  • You have a Fish Fountain in the garden.
  • Have a Juicygoose house in the garden.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it an oak tree seed changes its color to green.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink and Orange)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink and orange.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a banana changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Gooseberry seed to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Gooseberry to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • A cluckles' "comb"
  • Five feathers making a semi-circle from the back of the neck
  • Longer bill

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Resident

  • Feeding 1 gooseberry to a Quackberry evolves it into a Juicygoose.

Romance

  • Have a Fish Fountain in the garden.
  • Have 10 pinometers of water.
  • Has eaten 2 Newtgats.
  • Have a Juicygoose house in the garden.

Variant (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a jar of Peanut Butter.

Juicygoose Uses

  • Eating 2 Juicygeese helps to meet the Cocoadile's Romance requirements. (Original & Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Juicygoose helps to meet the Mallowolf's Romance requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)

Species Conflicts

Eaten by

  • Cocoadile (Romance Requirement: Original & PP)
  • Mallowolf (Romance Requirement: TiP only)

Scared of

  • Cocoadile

Rivals

  • Quackberry
  • Swanana

Other Information

Newtgats will not visit your garden if one or more Juicygeese is a resident.

Juicygeese are happier when sprinkled with water.

In the TV Series

J.J. Juicygoose

J.J. Juicygoose is a character in the TV series. He is a mix tape recorder who works for Hudson Horstachio.

Trivia

  • When you buy a trap, Langston claims, "That will almost definitely work, like Cocoadile jaws on Juicygoose candy!"
