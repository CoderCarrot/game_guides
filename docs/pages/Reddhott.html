Imagine scorching the furniture when you sit on it. Imagine having to move every few seconds because if you don't, the ground underneath you sets alight. What kind of life is that? Who is responsible for this?

The Reddhott is a red firefly piñata. It is the evolved form of the Taffly.

Heat and light are major factors in the Reddhott's life. Only by introducing a Taffly to a source of these things can you get hold of a Reddhott (not literally) in the first place, and romancing only triples the requirements.

Appearance

It looks like an orange and yellow Taffly covered in flames. It has bright blue wings and six small red legs.

Requirements (Original)

Resident Requirements

  • Directing a Taffly to a Firebrand to set it on fire and then quickly dousing it with the Watering Can evolves it into a Reddhott.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 chilis.
  • There are 3 lights of any kind in the garden.
  • Have a Reddhott house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a piece of Carrot Cake.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Resident Requirements

  • Directing a Taffly to a Firebrand to set it on fire and then quickly dousing it with the Watering Can evolves it into a Reddhott.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 chili.
  • Has eaten 2 radishes.
  • Has eaten 1 fireweed.
  • Have a Reddhott house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 3 Requirements (Red)

  • Has eaten a piece of Carrot Cake.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Chili seed to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Fireweed seed to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard 1: Wings that are red and have more of a flame design to them
  • Wildcard 2: "Mohawk" Down the Center of Body
  • Wildcard 3: Spiked spines on back

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Set a Taffly on fire, then douse it with your watering can.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Resident

  • Directing a Taffly to a Firebrand to set it on fire and then quickly dousing it with the Watering Can evolves it into a Reddhott.

Romance

  • Has eaten 3 rotten Chilis.
  • Have 3 lights of any kind in the garden.
  • Have a Reddhott house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell flower.

Reddhott Uses

  • Having 5 Reddhotts in the garden helps to meet the Dragumfly's Unblock requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Reddhott helps to meet the Salamango's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Reddhott helps to meet the Dragonache's Mature requirements.
  • Winning a fight against a Reddhott helps to meet the Dragumfly's Resident requirements.

Species conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten by

  • Salamango
  • Dragonache

Rivals

  • Dragumfly
  • Arocknid

Other information

  • Rather than breeding Reddhott piñata, it is far more cost-effective to breed Tafflies and evolve them if you wish to make money.
  • Reddhotts dislike being watered with the watering can.

Glitches and Errors

  • In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise, a wild Taffly may run into a Firebrand. If doused, it will become a resident Reddhott without ever becoming a resident Taffly.
  • In Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, a Reddhott may appear to still be on fire after it is doused. This glitch is only visual and goes away if the piñata is packed in a Crate and mailed.
