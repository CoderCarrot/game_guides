Cherrapin

This Piñata is perfectly adapted to its life. It has strong flippers to pull over land and through the water. It has strong jaws for feeding and it has a tough shell for... er. Well, obviously to protect it from... er birds. Yes birds flying overhead, carrying... anvils, and er... pianos.

The Cherrapin is a green sea turtle piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. with yellow highlights. It has a yellow shell covering most of its body.

Despite being a sea turtle, it is able to move on land by dragging its legs around and survive outside water. It is never in a hurry to get anywhere. This tough-shelled tortoise believes in taking his own sweet time - and we do mean sweet!

Requirements

Appear Requirements

  • You have 5 square pinometers of sand.
  • You have 5 square pinometers of water.
  • Have a water lily plant in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 7 square pinometers of sand.
  • You have 7 square pinometers of water.
  • Have 2 water lily plants in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 10 square pinometers of water.
  • You have 10 square pinometers of sand.
  • Has eaten 3 water lily flowers.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Cherrapin house in the garden.
  • Is wearing Belly-Splash Specials.
  • Has eaten a Lickatoad Egg.
1/3

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Blueberry Muffin will turn it blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it Honey will turn it yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Feeding it a Gooseberry will turn it green.

Trick Requirements

  • Has eaten a Watercress Seed.
  • Has eaten a Watercress Flower.
1/2

Wildcard Variants

  • Horn on snout
  • Square frills on edge of shell
  • Blue spikes on shell

Happiness Tips

  • Cherrapins like eating Green Flutterscotches, watercress, peas, gooseberries, and poison ivy.
  • Cherrapins dislike eating apples, venus pinata traps, roses, poppies, and radishes.
  • Cherrapins hate eating Custaceans.
