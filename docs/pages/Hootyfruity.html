The Hootyfruity is a brown owl piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It also appears in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise.

This bookish Piñata is wise beyond his years, but that doesn’t stop him from letting his feathers down and partying when he’s on the job!

Once you have made a Hootyfruity resident, you can find special arctic-color variants in the Piñarctic.

Appearance

It has a round brown body with yellow accents and a light-blue lower half. It also has very large blue eyes and a yellow beak.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • There is a Mousemallow house in the garden.
  • There is a Sherbat house in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • There are 5 Mousemallow residents in the garden.
  • There are 5 Sherbat residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Mousemallows.
  • Has eaten 2 Sherbats.

Romance Requirements

  • There is a Hootyfruity house in the garden.
  • Has eaten a Squazzil.
  • Is wearing Geek Glasses.
  • Has eaten 2 Lemmonings.
