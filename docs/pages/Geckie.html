This little lizard piñata enjoys the bright sunshine and sand between its toes. Of course no-one would find living in a desert much fun unless they had already caught a bit too much sun... and accidentally roasted their brain. I'm sorry, did I say that out loud?

The Geckie is a green horned lizard piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It can only be found in the Dessert Desert.

Appearance

It is a small green and yellow lizard with a pink tongue and spikes running down its back.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Can only be found in the Dessert Desert.

Bait Requirements

  • A Geckie finds hard to resist a delicate snowdrop.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 2 square pinometers of sand (2%).
  • Has eaten 1 Flutterscotch.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Mothdrop.
  • Have 3 square pinometers of sand (3%).
  • Have a Geckie house in the garden.
1/2

Variant 1 Requirements (Orange)

  • Feeding it a pumpkin changes its color to orange.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a cactus flower changes its color to pink.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a fireweed changes its color to blue.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Raisant to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it a Watercress to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Three horns on head
  • Fin on tail
  • Crest on neck

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Flutterscotch resident reward.

Geckie Uses

  • Having 3 Geckies in the garden helps meet the Badgesicle Visit requirements.
  • Eating 1 Geckie helps meet the Badgesicle Resident requirements.
  • Eating 1 Geckie helps meet the Macaraccoon Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Geckie helps mature the Choclodocus.

Happiness Tips

  • Geckies love eating blueberries, gooseberries, and apples.
  • They also love hunting Mothdrops.
  • Geckies dislike eating yams and being sprinkled by the watering can.
