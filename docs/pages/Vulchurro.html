Vulchurro

Vulchurros tend to have a bad name, but if you think about it, they are planet savers. They are a totally organic, completely natural, self-contained, recycling plant. You don't even need to take the 'trash' out, they will come to you.

The Vulchurro is a pink vulture piñata introduced in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. It can only be found in the Dessert Desert.

Appearance

It is a pink vulture pinata. It has a boxy head with green irises and yellow eyelids. The tip of its beak is yellow, and the tip at the bottom droops out lower than the rest of the head.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 12 gardener or better.
  • Can only be found in the Dessert Desert.

Trap Requirements

  • A Vulchurro likes to eat something to make it sweat.
Note: Bait the trap with a Chili.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 15 square pinometers of sand (15%).
  • Has eaten 2 Bones.
  • Has eaten 1 Crowla.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Jellies.
  • You have 20 square pinometers of sand (20%).
  • Have a Cactus in the garden.
  • Have a Vulchurro house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Bluebell Seed.

Variant 2 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten a Pea.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten a Water Lily flower.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Cactus flower to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Buzzenge to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Longer beak, like a toucan
  • Larger tail
  • Feathers on head

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Vulchurros cannot be attracted in Just for Fun mode, except you can use the Post Office.

Other Information

  • Bunnycombs, Crowlas, Custaceans, Lemmonings, Mousemallows, S'morepions, Sherbats, Squazzils, Tartridges and Buzzenges will not visit your garden if one or more Vulchurros is a resident.
  • Vulchurros are quite useful for getting rid of Buzzenges, Crowlas, Custaceans, Lemmonings, and Sherbats.
  • Vulchurros are scared of and will run away from Tigermisus and Roarios.
  • Vulchurros dislike being watered with the Watering Can.
  • Vulchurros will start fights with Sprinkling

In the TV Series

An old Vulchurro appears in the TV series episodes The Old Piñatas Home, The Transparent Trap, and The Fudgetive. In The Fudgetive he is seen trying to sell products to Fergy Fudgehog in which he ends up buying something from him to avoid getting caught. Later in the episode, he sells Fergy a piñata catapult to reach Professor Pester's mountain, which he ends up destroying.

Sometime between The Fudgetive and The Old Piñata Home, he retired.
