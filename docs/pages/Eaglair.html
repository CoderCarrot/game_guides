Eaglair

The mightiest of the birds of prey, the Eaglair has earned respect through its natural nobility, tempered strength, and thumping great talons.

Level

9

Base Value

4500 coins

Housing

Eaglair Home

Animal

Bald Eagle

Candy

Éclair

Attack Type

Binoculars
The Eaglair is a red, green, and purple eagle piñata. Its beak is yellow and it has green eyelids. The part of its body that is near its face is a light pink or red color, while further away from its head its body is more of a red color, with a red pattern on it. Its wings are cyan with purple stripes. Its stubby feet are yellow.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • You are a level 33 gardener or better.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 5 different species as residents in the garden

Resident Requirements

  • Have an oak tree in the garden
  • Has eaten 4 Buzzenges
  • Have 15 different species as residents in the garden

Helpful tip for meeting the last two requirements: Hunt plenty of Buzzenges to feed on, then hunt / evolve all ten species of Flutterscotch. Fill the rest of the species as needed from Paper Pets or by placing things in your garden that'll attract low-level piñatas

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 3 Sweetooths
  • Have 2 fully grown oak trees in the garden
  • Have an Eaglair house in the garden

Variant 1 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a buttercup seed changes its color to yellow.

Variant 2 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 3 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it a bottle of milk changes its color to white.

Note: Each of the three color variants drops the base value of the Eaglair to only 100 chocolate coins.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • Have a fully grown oak tree in the garden.

Unblock Requirements

  • Have 5 Elephanillas to scare away the Ruffian.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 5 different species as residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Parrybos.
  • Have 10 different species as residents in the garden.
  • Has eaten 2 Cinnamonkeys.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Galagoogoos.
  • Have 2 fully grown oak trees in the garden.
  • Have an Eaglair house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell Seed changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (White)

  • Feeding it a bottle of milk changes its color to white.

Variant 3 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a buttercup seed changes its color to yellow.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a bird of paradise to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a sunflower to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Toucan Sam beak
  • Feather tuft "ears" on head
  • Crown-like structure from top of the beak

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Parrybo resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 9 gardener or better.

Visit

  • You have 5 different species as residents.

Resident

  • Have a fully-grown Oak Tree in the garden.
  • Has eaten 4 Buzzenges.
  • Have 5 different species as residents.

Romance

  • Has eaten 3 Sweetooths.
  • Have 2 fully-grown Oak Trees in the garden.
  • Have an Eaglair house in the garden.

Variant (White)

  • Has eaten a bottle of Milk.

Eaglair Uses

  • Eating 1 Eaglair helps to meet the Roario's Romance requirements. (Note: Roario needs to eat a single piñata which costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance.)
  • Eating 1 Eaglair helps to meet the Tigermisu's Romance requirements. (Pocket Paradise)

Glitches and Errors

  • The Encyclopedia in the original Viva Piñata erroneously states that it must drink 4 bottles of milk to change to its white variant, when it actually only needs to drink 1.

Other Information

Eaglairs are very advanced piñatas, worth nearly 5000 chocolate coins. It will take some work to get one, due to the second resident requirement, but you may as well pay any price through Gretchen Fetchem’s to get one, as you’ll be able to recoup the money when you start selling off your Eaglair offspring.

In the original Viva Piñata the Wildcard Eaglair is identical in appearance to the regular Eaglair.

In the TV Series

Eddie Eaglair

Eddie Eaglair is a character in the Viva Piñata TV series. He is a drill sergeant hired by Langston Lickatoad to get Paulie Pretztail and Fergy Fudgehog sent to a party.
