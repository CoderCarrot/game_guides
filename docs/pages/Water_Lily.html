Water Lily, known as "Waterlily" in Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, is a flower that can be grown on mud. You can purchase water lily seeds from Costolot's General Store, or try talking with Seedos to get a water lily seed. You unlock it at level 15.

How best to grow it

Use 3 applications of purple fertilizer immediately after planting it, for maximum effect.

Uses

Water lily seed uses

  • Feeding a water lily seed to a Arocknid, Candary, Chewnicorn, Cocoadile, Crowla, Goobaa, Juicygoose, Lackatoad, Macaracoon, Mallowolf, Newtgat, Ponocky, Quackberry, Squazzil, Whirlm, or White Flutterscotch changes its color to a pink variant.
  • Feeding a water lily seed and a snapdragon seed to a Moozipan changes its color to a pale pink variant.
  • Feeding a water lily seed and a Bird of Paradise flower to a Chippopatamus changes it to a pink and purple variant.

Water lily uses

  • 1 water lily in the garden helps meet the Sweetooth Visit requirements.
  • Eating 1 water lily helps meet the Goobaa Romance requirements.
  • Eating 2 water lilies helps meet the Sweetooth Resident requirements.
  • Eating 3 water lilies helps meet the Swanana Romance requirements.
  • Eating 10 water lilies helps meet the Chippopatamus Resident requirements.
  • 14 water lilies in the garden helps meet the Chippopatamus Visit requirements.
  • Feeding a gem tree seed, water lily, and a poppy to a Pigxie changes its color to a fuchsia variant.
  • Feeding a water lily to a Kittyfloss, Pretztail, Raisant, Reddhott, or Sparrowmint changes its color to a pink variant.
  • Feeding honey and a water lily flower to a Badgesicle changes its color to a fuchsia variant.
