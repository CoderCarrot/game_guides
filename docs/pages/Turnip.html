The turnip is a purple bulb vegetable with green leaves on top.

Mousemallows are attracted to turnips. They are one of the choices of seeds that Seedos offers in Viva Piñata and Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, the other being the Carrot.

Appearance

They have a lighter purple pattern near the bottom of the bulb.

Uses

Requirements (Original)

  • Eating a Turnip is required to make a Mousemallow a resident

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

  • Eating a Turnip is required to make a Mousemallow a resident

Variants

  • Eating a Turnip will turn a Whirlm purple
