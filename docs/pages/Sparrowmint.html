Sparrowmint

If someone told you they had never seen a Sparrowmint, they must have never opened their eyes, or been green with a ray gun. Now only if there was a good use for them.

The Sparrowmint is a small brown sparrow piñata.

As a small bird, it can fly onto other larger piñatas, tree branches, buildings, and certain decorations. It can also fly over most fences, so a bird proof fence is required to block off this piñata from accessing a certain zone.

Once Whirlms populate a garden, you'll find Sparrowmints close behind. Given a steady food source, they'll stick around, and are easily evolved into something much more exotic.

Appearance

It is the average bird shape. Its beak is pink, its eyelid is dark brown, and it has green irises. It has a small feathery tail, small brown wings, a white and brown pattern on its belly, and orange feet.

Requirements (Original)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a Buttercup flower to a Sparrowmint evolves it into a Candary.

Appear Requirements

  • Have 1 Whirlm resident in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 2 Whirlm residents in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • After 2 Whirlms have romanced.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Whirlm.
  • Have a Sparrowmint house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten 1 Thistle flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten 1 Water Lily flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten 1 Watercress flower.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Evolution Requirements

  • Feeding a Dandelion flower (instead of a buttercup) to a Sparrowmint evolves it into a Candary.

Appear Requirements

  • Have 1 Whirlm resident in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • Have 1 Whirlm resident in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Whirlm.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 Bluebell seed.
  • Have a Sparrowmint house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Purple)

  • Has eaten 1 Thistle flower.

Variant 2 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has eaten 1 Water Lily flower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Green)

  • Has eaten 1 Watercress flower.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it a Daisy Seed to have it perform trick 1.
  • Feed it a Daisy flower to have it perform trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Wildcard 1: Ibex-like Horns
  • Wildcard 2: "Mohawk" Feathers on Head
  • Wildcard 3: Three sets of wings
