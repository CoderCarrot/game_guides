The Moozipan is a domestic tan and pink cow piñata.

In Viva Piñata, it is sold for 3080 chocolate coins at Miss Petula's Paper Pets shop in the village starting at level 25. In Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise Paper Pets doesn't exist and it is instead purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's. In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise it is found in the wild instead, and thus has appearance, visit, resident requirements to meet.

It is able to produce Milk if fed a Sunflower in Viva Piñata or Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, or a Joy Candy in Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. After producing milk, its udders will be engorged in Viva Piñata and Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise. In Viva Piñata: Pocket Paradise, instead of their udders changing, a milk icon will appear above Moozipans that are ready to be milked. It can be milked at Milking Shed. If the Moozipan is accessorized with a Bell it will automatically produce milk by itself and automatically go into the Milking Shed. The Flapyak can produce milk in the same matter.

A sufficient lifestyle makes the Moozipan a popular domestic Piñata. It doesn't hurt that it's almost criminally easy to keep - no diva demands here, some grass, flowers and a banana split to snack on will do fine.

Appearance

It is a boxy cow piñata. Its mouth and nose area is pink, with a yellow ring pattern next to it, it has black eyelashes, yellow horns, and long ears. Its body is boxy, with long legs, and it also has a saddle like darker tan pattern near the back of its body and its cow tail, which ends slightly larger than the base. It also has dark tan spots on its body. It has pink udders on its belly, with long teats drooping from its udders.

When it is ready to be milked, its udders and teats will start flashing white and be larger than normal.

Requirements (Original)

The Moozipan cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Paper Pets's Piñata category for 3080 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 25. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance Requirements

  • Have 12 daisies in the garden OR have 12 buttercups in the garden.
  • Has eaten 1 banana split.
  • Have 180 square pinometers of long grass (18%).
  • Have a Moozipan house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a sandwich changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Banana Seed changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily Seed and a snapdragon seed changes its color to pink.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You have 5 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Have a milk churn in the garden.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 10 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Have a milk churn in the garden.
  • Have 10 buttercups in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • You have 15 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Has eaten 5 buttercups.
  • Has eaten 2 gooseberry fools.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten 1 banana split.
  • Has eaten 4 dandelions.
  • Have 18 square pinometers of long grass.
  • Have a Moozipan house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a sandwich changes its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it a Banana Seed changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily Seed and a snapdragon seed changes its color to pink.

Trick Requirements

  • Feed it Milk to have it perform Trick 1.
  • Feed it Cheese to have it perform Trick 2.

Wildcard Variants

  • Long twisting horns
  • Long ears
  • Three large beads on tail

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having 10 square pinometers (1%) of long grass in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

The Moozipan cannot be found in the wild. Instead, it must be purchased from Gretchen Fetchem's Pets category for 2800 Chocolate Coins after reaching Gardener level 5. As such, it has no Appear, Visit, or Resident requirements.

Romance

  • Have 180 pinometers of long grass (18%).
  • Has eaten 1 Banana Split.
  • Have 12 Daisies grown in the garden OR have 12 Buttercups grown in the garden.
  • Have a Moozipan house in the garden.

Variant (Blue)

  • Has eaten a Sandwich.

Moozipan Uses

  • Feeding a sunflower to a Moozipan and building a Milking Shed will allow it to make milk. In Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise, the Moozipan instead needs to eat a Joy Candy before it will be ready to produce milk.
  • Giving a Moozipan a Bell will allow it to make milk automatically, without eating a sunflower.

In the TV Series

Multiple Moozipan characters appear in the television series, as well as a robot named the Moozitron with the appearance of the Moozipan. A Moozipan is able to serve milk directly from its udder. Moozipans are also able to serve diary products such as milkshakes directly from their udder as well. In addition, Moozipan characters usually stand up like a biped creature, unlike in the game series where they always walk on four legs.

Mabel Moozipan

Mabel Moozipan is a major character in the TV series. She is a gardener who holds a hoe.

The Moozipan Dancers

The Moozipan Dancers are a group of Moozipans who appear in the episode Hudson's Holiday.

The Moozitron

The Moozitron is a remote controlled robot designed by Professor Pester that looks like a Moozipan. Using his voice, the robot identified himself as "Maurice Moozipan", a male Moozipan, in an attempt to start a political movement with the intention of destroying the Cannoñata.
