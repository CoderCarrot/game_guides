Sweetooth

Sweetooths like playing table tennis. They are surprisingly skillful with their broad paddle-like tails which they use to return almost any shot you can fire at them. Their lightning fast reactions make the Sweetooth a formidable foe at the tennis table!

Level

6

Base Value

2100 coins

Housing

Sweetooth Home

Animal

Beaver

Candy

Sweet Tooth, which is an idiom meaning a fondness of sweets

Attack Type

Handsaws
The Sweetooth is a brown and blue beaver piñata.

No need to worry for your fences and trees, as Piñata Island Sweetooths aren't really into property damage. However, they are voracious herbivores and waddle just a bit too slowly to evade those gnashing Sours.

Appearance

Its head is brown, its eyelids are indigo, and it has light blue irises. It has a light blue stripe that wraps around its body, and covers its forelegs, and its lower body is orange, with an indigo pattern. It has a large, round, flat tail that is covered in a pattern of various shades of blue, and indigo. The Sweetooth also visibly has two large, buck teeth.

  • You are a level 21 gardener or better.

Visit Requirements

  • You have 120 square pinometers of water (12%).
  • Have 1 Water Lily in the garden OR have 1 Fir Tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten 2 Water Lily flowers.

  • Has eaten a Sunflower.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Has drunk a bottle of Medicine.

Sweetooth Uses

  • Having 5 Sweetooths in the garden helps to meet the Fizzlybear's Unblock requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Sweetooth helps to meet the Sour Cocoadile's Resident requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 3 Sweetooths helps to meet the Eaglair's Romance requirements. (Original and Pocket Paradise)
  • Eating 1 Sweetooth helps meet the Cocoadile's Resident requirements. (Trouble in Paradise)

Trivia

  • The Sweetooth resembles Gnawty the beaver from the Banjo-Kazooie series.
  • In Trouble in Paradise, the Sweetooth information blurb has a grammatical error: "Lightning" is spelled as "lightening".

In the TV Series

Dr. Sweetooth

A character in the TV series who only appears in Franklingestion. He & his crew helped Franklin with his regurgitating vegetables instead of candy.

Silvia Sweetooth

Hudson's makeup artist who first appears in Wild Horstachios.

Sal & Santo Sweetooth

Sal & Santo are dam builders & construction workers who debut in Hudson Tells All. They are parodies of Mario & Luigi, complete with stereotypical Italian accents.
