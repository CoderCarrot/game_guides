Symbolizing purity, this mythical creature can only be seen by innocent maidens. It is one of the most beautiful creatures...probably, if I could see it.

The Chewnicorn is a rare toothpaste white unicorn piñata. It is able to heal sick piñatas.

Appearance

It has a brown and gold unicorn horn on top of its head, its hair is a mix of blue and cyan sheets, and its mouth is yellow. Its legs are orange, its tail is orange and red, and it has a yellow neck band pattern. Its irises are blue.

Requirements (Original)

Appear Requirements

  • You have the Horstachio Master Romancer award.

Visit Requirements

  • Have a gem tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten fifteen gems.
  • Have a fully grown gem tree in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten three rainbow gems.
  • Have a Chewnicorn house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a water lily seed changes its color to pink.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it an ear of corn changes its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a water lily and a bluebell changes its color to blue.

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise)

Appear Requirements

  • You have the Horstachio Master Romancer award.

Visit Requirements

  • Have a gem tree in the garden.

Resident Requirements

  • Has eaten nine gems.
  • Have a fully grown gem tree in the garden.

Romance Requirements

  • Has eaten three rainbow gems.
  • Have a Chewnicorn house in the garden.

Variant 1 Requirements (Blue)

  • Feeding it a Bluebell will change its color to blue.

Variant 2 Requirements (Yellow)

  • Feeding it an ear of corn will change its color to yellow.

Variant 3 Requirements (Pink)

  • Feeding it a Water Lily Seed will change its color to pink.

Trick Requirements

  • Trick 1: Feed it an Orchid Flower.
  • Trick 2: Feed it Jam.

Wildcard Variants

  • Curled horn
  • Long ears
  • Two extra horns

Requirements (Trouble in Paradise - Just for Fun)

  • Attract it by having a Horstachio resident in the garden.

Requirements (Pocket Paradise)

Appear

  • You are a level 6 gardener or better.
  • Have a Gem Tree in the garden.

Visit

  • You have the Horstachio Master Romancer Award.

Resident

  • Have a fully grown Gem Tree in the garden.
  • Has eaten 15 gems.

Romance

  • Has eaten three rainbow gems.
  • Have a Chewnicorn house in the garden.

Variant (Yellow)

  • Has eaten a Chili.

Chewnicorn Uses

  • 1 Chewnicorn in the garden helps to meet the Galagoogoo's Romance requirements.
  • Eating 1 Chewnicorn helps to meet the Roario's Romance requirements. (Note: A Roario needs to eat a single piñata which costs 4,500 chocolate coins or over to romance.)
  • Directing a Chewnicorn to a sick piñata will heal it and earn the player an achievement.

Species Conflicts

Eats

  • None

Eaten by

  • Roario
  • Tigermisu

Scared of

  • Roario (Avoids Garden if Resident)
  • Tigermisu (Avoids Garden if resident)

Rivals

  • Horstachio
  • Ponocky
  • Roario
  • Zumbug

Other Information

  • Although Chewnicorns do not like or dislike being watered with the watering can, they will still harbor a startled reaction while the watering can is held near them.
